Start Your Career in Planning & Coordination
We are currently seeking an Entry Level Event & Travel Planning Coordinator to support the organization of event details and travel-related arrangements. This role is designed for individuals who are eager to learn, highly organized, and interested in developing skills in planning and coordination.

No prior experience is required—training and support are provided to help you succeed in a structured, remote environment.

What You’ll Be Involved In


Assisting with event planning tasks and travel coordination
Organizing schedules, timelines, and planning details
Supporting the collection of client preferences and requirements
Tracking tasks to ensure all plans stay on schedule
Communicating updates, confirmations, and key information
Maintaining organized digital records and planning systems




Skills That Set You Up For Success


Strong attention to detail and willingness to learn
Good communication and follow-up skills
Ability to stay organized and manage multiple tasks
Self-motivated with a positive, team-oriented mindset
Basic comfort with computers and online tools




Training & Support



Step-by-step onboarding process
Ongoing guidance and resources
Structured systems to help you build confidence and skills




What You Can Expect


Flexible remote / work-from-home environment
Opportunity to grow into more advanced coordination roles
Supportive and collaborative team atmosphere
Skill development in planning, scheduling, and organization




Work Setup
This is a remote role requiring a reliable internet connection and the ability to stay organized while working independently.
