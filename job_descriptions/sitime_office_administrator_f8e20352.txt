About SiTime

SiTime is the Precision Timing company.

Timing is the heartbeat of all electronics, ensuring performance, resilience and scalability. For decades, quartz devices, non-silicon technology, have kept systems in sync, but they struggle in harsher, more demanding environments. MEMS-based Precision Timing delivers greater accuracy, smaller size and resilience. Today, MEMS timing powers over 400 applications, including high-growth ones in AI datacenters, automated driving, industrial and humanoid robots, wearables and IoT.

Our semiconductor MEMS programmable solutions offer a rich feature set that enables customers to differentiate their products with higher performance, smaller size, lower power, and better reliability. With more than 4 billion devices shipped, SiTime is changing the timing industry. For more information, visit: www.sitime.com .

Job Summary

The Office/HR Administrator is responsible for organizing and coordinating office operations and procedures in order to ensure organizational effectiveness, efficiency, and safety. The ideal candidate enjoys providing great service and is able to work independently with little or no supervision. This person must be a self-starter, exceedingly well organized, resourceful, and flexible.

SiTime Finland Oy offers flexible working hours with possibility of part-time work.

Responsibilities


Ensure that SiTime Finland is a comfortable and productive place to work – from managing facilities and vendor relationships to making sure our snacks and supplies are in stock, etc.;
Welcome visitors and incoming calls in a professional manner;
Manage office shipments and deliveries;
Set up conference calls, meetings (on-site and off-site), presentations, etc.;
Provide new hires with the badge, parking permit etc.;
Assist travel management;
Assist financial accounting with local partners;
Align company HR guidelines with local practices;
Coordinate with landlord on safety
Manage correspondence and documentation as needed


Qualifications & Requirements


At least 2 years of experience as an office coordinator or manager within a small to medium-size company;
BA/BS Degree or above;
Strong knowledge of English, Finish is fluent


Desired Characteristics & Attributes


Strong ability to multi-task and prioritize while providing quality customer service to employees;
Good communication skills
