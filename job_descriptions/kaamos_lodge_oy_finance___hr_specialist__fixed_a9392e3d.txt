Explore the North – join a growing travel company

Explore the North is an Arctic tourism and hospitality company founded in northern Sweden, now operating across both Swedish and Finnish Lapland. Our lodges are mainly located in the beautiful Tornedalen (Torne Valley) region — a unique cross-border area close to the Arctic Circle, where two cultures and breathtaking Arctic nature meet.

Looking for a role where you can truly make an impact on both finance and HR? With us, youll combine numbers and people – and see the results of your work in practice.

Were now looking for a Finance & HR Specialist to support our business in a diverse and responsible role. This position is a fixed-term parental leave replacement approximately one year. The position will start in mid- or late August.

What youll be doing

Finance – keeping the big picture under control

Accounts payable and receivable
Month-end closing, reconciliations, and investigations
Financial analysis and reporting
Tax-related matters
Understanding of accounting
Acting as the key contact person for accounting

HR – supporting people and managers

Key user role for Maraplan and HR/recruitment systems
Supporting and coordinating recruitment processes
Assisting managers with employment contracts
Supporting challenging HR situations
Interpreting collective agreements
Acting as the contact point for payroll

What we expect from you

Relevant degree (e.g. Bachelor of Business Administration)
Experience in finance and/or HR tasks
Ability to work independently and manage overall processes
Strong communication and problem-solving skills
Fluent Finnish and English; Swedish is considered an advantage

Why join us?

A versatile role combining finance and HR
Opportunity to work in a growing and developing company
Flexible working model – mainly remote, with occasional team gatherings at our back office
A supportive and down-to-earth team where things get done together

Interested? Send your application and CV as soon as possible email with title Finance & HR Specialist to work@explorthenorth.se – we will review applications during the process.
