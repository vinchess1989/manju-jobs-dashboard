About The Company

DoorDash is a leading technology and logistics company dedicated to empowering local economies through innovative delivery solutions. Founded with the mission to connect people with the goods and services they need, DoorDash has rapidly grown into a global organization recognized for its commitment to customer satisfaction, operational excellence, and community impact. Our platform enables seamless delivery of food, groceries, and other essential items, partnering with local merchants and restaurants to foster economic growth and convenience for consumers worldwide. We foster a dynamic, inclusive, and collaborative environment where innovation thrives, and every team member is encouraged to contribute their unique perspectives. Our comprehensive benefits package, focus on diversity and inclusion, and commitment to employee well-being make DoorDash an exceptional place to build a meaningful career.

About The Role

We are seeking a highly organized and proactive Executive Assistant to support our Engineering leadership team. In this role, you will serve as a vital partner to our engineering executives, managing their calendars, coordinating team activities, and overseeing various projects to ensure operational efficiency. You will act as a trusted point of contact, handling sensitive information with discretion and professionalism. This position requires a detail-oriented individual who excels at multitasking, problem-solving, and fostering a positive team environment. The role offers a hybrid work schedule, with in-person presence required at our San Francisco headquarters or Century City office three days a week. As an integral part of the team, you will help promote team cohesion, facilitate effective communication, and contribute to the overall success of our engineering initiatives.

Qualifications


5+ years of experience in an administrative or executive support role
Expertise in G-suite tools including Google Calendar, Gmail, Docs, Spreadsheets, and Slides
Proven experience in event planning, project management, and budgeting
Background working in fast-paced, growing organizations such as tech startups or large technology companies
Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to diagnose issues and implement creative solutions
Exceptional organizational and time-management skills
Ability to handle highly confidential information with discretion
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
Adaptability to shifting priorities and environments


Responsibilities


Manage and maintain complex calendars for engineering leaders, scheduling meetings, and coordinating priorities
Organize and facilitate team-building activities, events, and off-sites to promote team cohesion
Assist in planning and overseeing team events, workshops, and project timelines
Coordinate multiple projects simultaneously, ensuring deadlines are met and resources are allocated effectively
Handle sensitive and confidential information with integrity and professionalism
Act as a liaison between engineering leaders and other departments or external partners
Support onboarding processes for new team members and assist with internal communications
Identify opportunities to improve operational workflows and implement innovative solutions
Promote a positive, inclusive team culture through proactive engagement and support
