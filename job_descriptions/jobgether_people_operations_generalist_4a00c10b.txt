Räätälöi ansioluetteloni
This position is listed on behalf of a partner company, who manages all applications and next steps. Our partner is looking for a People Operations Generalist based in the United States.

This role sits at the center of People Operations, combining HR systems ownership, payroll execution, employee support, and recruiting coordination in a highly tech-forward environment. You will act as the primary administrator of the HRIS while ensuring data integrity, workflow automation, and system optimization across the employee lifecycle. A key part of your impact will be delivering accurate and timely payroll while supporting employees with day-to-day HR inquiries and guidance. You will also contribute to recruiting operations, leveraging modern HR and AI-enabled tools to improve hiring efficiency and candidate experience. The position blends operational execution with process improvement, requiring both strong attention to detail and a proactive mindset for automation and optimization. This is a hands-on, cross-functional role where you help shape a smooth and scalable employee experience.

Accountabilities


Serve as the primary HRIS administrator, managing system configuration, troubleshooting, user support, and data integrity audits.
Design, optimize, and maintain automated HRIS workflows to improve efficiency across the employee lifecycle.
Own end-to-end payroll processing, ensuring accuracy, timeliness, and compliance with applicable regulations.
Generate and analyze HR and payroll reports to support data-driven decision-making.
Act as the main point of contact for employee inquiries related to policies, payroll, benefits, and HR processes.
Manage employee lifecycle processes including onboarding, offboarding, and leave administration.
Support benefits administration across medical, dental, vision, retirement, and insurance programs.
Assist with recruiting activities including sourcing, screening, scheduling, and candidate pipeline coordination.
Leverage ATS and AI-enabled recruiting tools to improve hiring workflows and efficiency.
Support compliance efforts by drafting HR policies, SOPs, and employee communications.
Contribute to employee engagement initiatives and culture-building programs.
Maintain confidentiality and ensure secure handling of sensitive HR data and processes.


Requirements


3-5 years of experience in Human Resources or People Operations roles.
Hands-on experience as an HRIS administrator (Paylocity or similar strongly preferred).
Proven experience independently managing payroll processing from end to end.
Strong familiarity with HR technology, ATS platforms, and modern recruiting tools.
Comfortable leveraging AI tools and automation to improve HR workflows and productivity.
Experience supporting recruiting functions such as sourcing, screening, and coordination.
Strong proficiency in Microsoft Office, particularly Excel, and familiarity with Google Workspace.
Excellent attention to detail, organizational skills, and ability to manage multiple priorities.
Strong problem-solving skills with the ability to pivot between tactical and process improvement work.
High level of professionalism, integrity, and discretion when handling sensitive information.
Ability to work independently in a remote, decentralized environment.
Preferred: Bachelor’s degree in HR, Business, or related field and/or HR certification.


Benefits

Paid time off and flexible remote work environment
Employee engagement and wellness initiatives
Professional development and growth opportunities
Exposure to modern HR technology and AI-driven tools
Opportunity to shape scalable HR systems and processes
