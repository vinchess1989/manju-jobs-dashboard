Rebl Eats is building the leading next generation food company. Founded 5 years ago, Rebl Eats has developed more than 30 products and already sold 6 million products across Finland. Today we create ready-to-eat meals and snacks, sold in supermarkets and food delivery. But this is only the beginning as we plan to grow our product portfolio further and expand internationally. The company is backed by Wolt founder Elias Aalto, among other prominent investors and was also selected in Slush 100's iconic competition, 2024. You might have seen our successful appearances in the investment TV-show “Leijonan Luola” and Suomalainen Menestysresepti.

www.rebleats.com

Join us, and make an impact as an Operations Coordinator (Full-time)

“Operations” in this role means all aspects connected to our food production facility in Herttoniemi. In a nutshell, you’ll ensure we produce the right quantities of products, at the right time and level of quality, and make sure they are delivered on time to the right customers. This role is a true startup growth accelerator role, combining both hands-on hustling tasks, running existing processes but also developing them further.

The Responsibilities Include


Planning production cycles and work lists
Optimizing ingredient and packaging purchases
Coordinating production staff
Coordinating orders and needs with our production and logistics partners.
Be the bridge between our office team and production team.
Supporting in customer service analysis and building better customer service processes
More complex tasks to be added based on competence (e.g building operational tools, automating workflows, or managing data systems to support scaling)


To say it short, you help us lead our daily operations and bring our production processes to the next level to support our growth and profitability.

Get ready for personal development accelerator.


We will prioritize motivation, skills, and attitude over experience. You could get to start as a coordinator and we hope to see you taking on more responsibility and significant roles as we grow together.
Relevant work experience (e.g. from operations, logistics, supply chain or food industry) is a plus. And relevant academic studies (e.g. from business, engineering or food science) as even a bigger plus. Some first professional work experience through internships or traineeships is a must.


The Skills We Are After


You enjoy working with Excel and data.
You are structured, extremely detail-oriented and precise
You ace in juggling multiple tasks in an organized manner, and can work under pressure without ending up in a panic mode.
We appreciate a proactive problem solver, who is not afraid to get their hands dirty when needed.
Good communication skills, energy and positive can-do personality
You are fully proficient in Finnish and in English (the work involves communicating in Finnish with suppliers, maintenance companies, and also ordering ingredients in Finnish)
0-3 years experience in operations


We offer you a chance to be part of a highly ambitious impact startup in an interesting growth stage. Creating positive impact and moving humanity forward, meal by meal. With us you have a chance to build an exciting career path and progress fast with real responsibilities and fast growing opportunities, much beyond what a traditional company could offer. If you enjoy a versatile, fun and challenging work environment, this is definitely for you.

Location: This role is located in our HQ in Herttoniemi, Sorvaajankatu 15, Helsinki and requires being in the office most of the days.

Benefits: Always some free food and delicious tastings at the office as you might have already guessed. Also a chance to visit our foodtruck in the coolest festivals this summer! Other benefits we will add as we grow and can afford to treat ourselves a bit more.

Join us to be on the right side of history!

Please also mention your salary request and the earliest possible starting date. We will go through applications already during the application period and will proceed with potential candidates swiftly!
