About Bath Planet Of Seattle

Bath Planet is a nationally recognized home remodeling company specializing in high-quality bathroom solutions. Our Seattle location serves homeowners across Western Washington, providing professional bathroom remodels that combine style, functionality, and value.

We’re building an Event Department to represent our brand at shows, festivals, and home improvement events in-person. This is a hands-on role: you will secure events, work the booth, generate leads, and ensure your team delivers results.

Position Overview

We are seeking a rockstar Events Coordinator to lead our event marketing efforts in Western Washington. You and your team will be responsible for generating new customer leads, managing promotional booths, and driving sales.

This role requires you to book events, coordinate participation, and lead your team onsite during every event to ensure maximum success.

Responsibilities


Book and secure events and shows for every weekend within your territory
Plan, coordinate, and execute event schedules for you and your team
Recruit, hire, train, and mentor a team of Event Demonstrators
Ensure booths are safely set up, staffed, and taken down at every event
Generate leads that translate into sales and measurable profit
Maintain inventory, track productivity, labor, and budgets weekly
Set clear goals for each event, week, and month, and ensure they are achieved
Maintain open, regular, and timely communication with leadership and other departments
Actively work events, engage with potential customers, and demonstrate Bath Planet products


Requirements


Proven team management and leadership experience
Customer service or B2C marketing experience
Recruiting & hiring experience
Outgoing personality: must enjoy talking with new people and engaging customers
Valid driver’s license and reliable vehicle
Ability to stand for extended periods at events
Basic computer literacy for administrative tasks and reporting
Must be available to work every weekend


Helpful Skills


Promotional marketing experience at trade shows or events a big plus


Benefits


Flexible work environment
Competitive base salary ($800–$1,200/week)
Commission opportunities for leads and appointments generated
Opportunity to lead a growing Event Department and make a measurable impact


Why Join Us


Be a key part of building Bath Planet’s presence in Western WA
Lead a team while actively working events
Earn a combination of base salary + commission
Flexible, dynamic, and fun work environment


Apply NOW! We are looking for ONE stellar candidate ready to book events, coordinate participation, and lead your team onsite during every event.
