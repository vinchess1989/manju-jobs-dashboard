Project Manager
Hae paikkaa

Do you thrive on taking complex software projects from kickoff to go-live? Are you the person who keeps delivery on track, on time, and on scope, and whom customers trust to get it done? 

We are looking for a Project Manager to join us. In this role, you lead the delivery of Valamis, our digital learning platform, to companies and other organizations, and you take ownership of each project's successful delivery. You own scope, schedule, budget, and quality, coordinate cross-functional teams of specialists, and act as the main point of contact for our customers. 

This is an excellent opportunity to make a real impact in a global environment where your work directly supports our customers' and the company's success. At Valamis we have a great variety of customers, from Procter & Gamble to Adelaide Airport and the Jägermeister. 

What are we looking for in our new colleague? 

Experience and skills 

Experience delivering complex systems like ERP or other enterprise software, and a track record of getting them done. This matters most to us. 

A practical, hands-on way of running projects. You mix agile and more traditional ways of working to fit each project, rather than sticking to one fixed method. 

You're comfortable owning scope, schedule, budget, and quality across several projects at once. 

Strong stakeholder and customer management. 

Fluent English, spoken and written. 

Soft skills 

Confident and decisive. You drive projects forward instead of waiting. 

Clear communication with customers, your team, and stakeholders. 

Well organised, able to keep many moving parts in order. 

Calm and clear-headed when projects get demanding. 

An eye for detail. Small things don't slip past you. 

A reliable team player. 

It is a plus if you also have 

Experience delivering SaaS projects in a customer-facing role. 

A good technical understanding of how enterprise systems are built and integrated. 

Finnish language skills are a bonus, but not mandatory for the role. 

We support you in your development in many ways. For example, we have our own mentoring program and we use our own digital learning environment. In addition, we have internal presentations every now and then with technology and solution-oriented topics.  

Because the well-being and development of our employees is really important to us, we use the Humbol software that helps employees and supervisors have more meaningful conversations regularly. These discussions help us guide and support your career growth in the best possible way. We have a diverse and inclusive workplace, we organise events and all kinds of fun activities together, and we provide comprehensive occupational health care to support your overall well-being. 

If you want to know a little bit about your future colleagues, take a look at our career stories on our website. You can also explore our corporate culture through Instagram. 

We work in a hybrid model with three days a week at the office. We've found that being in the same room is where our best work happens. Ideas develop faster, problems get solved together, and it's easier to build the trust that successful project delivery relies on.
