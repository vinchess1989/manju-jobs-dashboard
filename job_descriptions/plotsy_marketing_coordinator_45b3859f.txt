Plotsy introduces the Plotsy vBook™, a fourth book format alongside print, eBook, and audiobook. We translate written books into cinematic, episodic video experiences built for the way people consume content today.




The Role

We're hiring a Marketing Coordinator to run paid media campaigns, manage our social presence, and keep our launch campaigns on track. You'll execute against strategy and briefs set by leadership as part of a small, fast-moving team. This is a hands-on, in-the-platforms role.

Reports to: Founder & Chief Publishing Officer




Responsibilities

Build, launch, and optimize campaigns on Google, YouTube, and Meta
Serve as the day-to-day liaison with our Amazon DSP agency, relaying briefs and pulling reports
Manage posting, scheduling, and community engagement across social channels
Write social captions, ad copy variations, and light email copy
Light design work using production-provided graphics
Maintain the content calendar and campaign tracker
Execute against templated launch campaign plans alongside leadership and production
Report on campaign performance weekly and flag what's working and what isn't
Read the titles you're actively marketing to bring real context to campaigns and content




Must-Haves

Hands-on experience buying and placing ads on Google and YouTube
Hands-on experience buying and placing ads on Meta
Experience managing brand social media accounts day to day
Strong written communication skills, clear and on-voice




Fit

U.S.-based, remote
Comfortable executing without a lot of hand-holding in a small team
Available for some overlap with Central time, where our leadership team is based
A genuine love of books and storytelling across genres




Nice-to-Haves

Background in entertainment, streaming, or subscription media
Experience with performance reporting and UTM tracking
Canva experience a plus




This is a contract-to-hire position. The role begins as an independent contractor engagement with the intent to convert to full-time employment based on performance and business needs.
