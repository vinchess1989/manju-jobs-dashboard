Räätälöi ansioluetteloni
Standard Metrics is an AI-driven financial data platform that helps investors and their portfolio companies make more informed, forward-looking decisions with automated reporting and benchmarking tools. We're a team of optimistic product builders on a mission to accelerate innovation in the private markets. Standard Metrics is backed by 8VC and Spark Capital, along with other leading VCs and angels, and is currently a trusted partner for many of the top VC/PE firms in the world.

Come Build With Us

As an Event Marketer at Standard Metrics, you will play a key role in building relationships with our customers, prospects, partners, and broader entrepreneurial community through high-quality, thoughtfully executed events. You'll be responsible for bringing our community together in person and creating experiences that support our sales, marketing, customer success, recruiting, and partnership efforts.

You'll own external events end-to-end, from early planning through on-site execution and post-event follow-up. This is a highly cross-functional, hands-on role for someone who thrives in fast-paced environments and enjoys seeing projects through from start to finish.

Travel is a core part of this role, as in-person presence is essential to delivering successful events.

What You'll Do


Plan, organize, and execute external events for current and prospective customers, users of our product, and other members of our community
Own the full event lifecycle, including concept, timeline, budget, logistics, vendor coordination, and on-site execution
Manage all event-related correspondence, including invitations, RSVPs, confirmations, reminders, and day-of communications
Partner closely with sales, marketing, and customer success to align events with pipeline development, customer engagement, and partnership goals
Collaborate directly with customers and external partners to co-host events
Track event details, attendance, and outcomes, ensuring information is accurately documented and shared internally
Lead post-event follow-up, including internal debriefs and reporting
Build and refine event processes, templates, and playbooks as the events program continues to scale


What You'll Bring


3-5+ years of experience planning, organizing, and executing external events for a B2B AI or SaaS company
Ability to manage multiple events, timelines, and stakeholders simultaneously
Comfort working cross-functionally with sales, marketing, and customer success teams
Willingness and ability to travel to support events, including 3+ events per quarter
A proactive, ownership-driven mindset with strong follow-through
Experience working in a fast-paced or startup environment
Experience partnering with customers or external partners on events
Familiarity with CRM systems (specifically Salesforce),event management tools, Slack, and Notion
Experience and creativity around automating operational workflows using AI tools
Knowledge of venture capital, private equity, or private markets, and comfort working with VC and investor-focused audiences

The estimated annual salary for this role is between $105,000-$130,000, plus a competitive equity package. Actual compensation will be determined based on experience and qualifications.

Health and dental insurance: We cover you and your dependents' medical/dental/vision insurance 100% in the USA. Internationally we match local health coverage for you and your family.

Paid parental leave: 12 weeks of paid leave for all new parents in the USA. Internationally we match parental leave standards in your area.
