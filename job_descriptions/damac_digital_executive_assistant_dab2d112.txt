We are seeking a highly organized, proactive, and high-performing Executive Assistant to support the SVP and the Europe Projects PMO. This role will act as the operational “glue” of the regional project ecosystem—ensuring that leadership priorities, governance rhythms, communications, and administrative workflows run seamlessly across multiple countries and project sites. The successful candidate will operate in a fast-paced, high-trust environment, enabling effective decision-making, coordination, and execution at leadership level.




Key Responsibilities

Executive & Leadership Support

Provide high-trust, high-pace executive support to the SVP, managing complex schedules, priorities, and communications.
Act as the central coordination point for leadership interactions across the Europe Projects function.
Triage and manage internal and external communications on behalf of the SVP and leadership team.

PMO Operations & Governance

Drive and manage key operational cadences, including:
Weekly project reviews
Steering committee sessions
Leadership touchpoints
Governance routines
Ensure smooth execution of PMO processes and alignment across regions and workstreams.

Project Coordination & Tracking

Track leadership actions, key decisions, risks, dependencies, and commitments across stakeholders.
Proactively follow up to ensure closure, accountability, and delivery.
Maintain a clear and reliable “single source of truth” for project and leadership tracking.

Reporting & Documentation

Coordinate the production of leadership reporting packs:
Consolidate inputs from multiple teams and countries
Manage timelines, deadlines, and version control
Ensure accuracy, clarity, and consistency of outputs
Prepare concise briefing materials and decision-ready summaries for the SVP and leadership team.

Cross-Functional Alignment

Work closely with cross-functional teams including:
Planning
Project Controls
Cost & Contracts
Document Control
Regional Delivery Leads
Ensure effective coordination and communication across all stakeholders.

Document Control & Administration

Support and oversee document control processes in collaboration with document controllers.
Ensure accurate maintenance of project records and documentation across systems.

Skills & Experience

Proven experience as an Executive Assistant or in a similar role supporting senior leadership in a project-driven or PMO environment.
Strong organisational and coordination skills with the ability to manage multiple priorities across regions.
Experience supporting large-scale, multi-country project environments is highly desirable.
Familiarity with PMO tools, project tracking systems, and reporting structures is an advantage.
Excellent stakeholder management and communication skills.
High attention to detail with the ability to synthesise complex information into clear outputs.
Ability to work independently, with discretion and sound judgment in a fast-paced environment.

Language Requirements

Fluent in Finnish and English (mandatory)
Additional languages such as Arabic, Swedish, Spanish, Italian, or Greek are a strong advantage
