Räätälöi ansioluetteloni
Our world depends on rocks, minerals, metals and other raw materials – the building blocks of modern society. As an industry leader, Metso helps transform these essential materials to enable sustainable modern life.

Our solutions and services support everything from renewable energy and electrification to the infrastructure and digital economy shaping our future. Together, we help our customers meet growing demand while driving the industry toward a more sustainable use of the earth’s natural resources.

Join nearly 18,000 Metsonites worldwide, each bringing unique expertise and experience to positive change. At Metso, you’re supported by a growth culture built on safety, wellbeing, inclusion and collaboration.

This is your opportunity to make an impact and rise above the possible.

Introduction

We are seeking a Trainee for the Legal team to support the Legal Executive Assistant in various administrative tasks, as well as to assist the Corporate Legal team with corporate legal and subsidiary governance related work. You will help to ensure that the team members have the best possible practical arrangements and conditions to focus on their areas of responsibility. You will report to the Legal Executive Assistant. The contract is approximately 6 months and, depending on the chosen trainee, is either part-time or full-time or a mixture of both. Our wish is that the trainee would start as soon as possible or in August at the latest.

As an Assistant Trainee, your daily tasks will consist of:



Supporting in subsidiary governance document management
Maintaining the Corporate Governance database and providing reports
Scheduling and organizing team’s face-to-face and virtual meetings, events, workshops, and trainings
Assisting with invoice handling, cost follow-up and travel management
Assisting with document management and information sharing (updating intranet, corporate registers, etc.)
Assisting other Legal and Compliance team members when needed



Who you are:



A service-minded and positive can-do attitude, with willingness to help others
Great teamworking skills while also ability to work independently
High level of integrity with the ability to handle confidential information
Good organizational skills and ability to multitask
Willingness to work in a quickly changing atmosphere, with tolerance for changing plans and time pressure
Ability to see the big picture without losing attention to detail
Excellent verbal and written communication skills in Finnish and English
Good command of Microsoft Office tools
Willingness to learn new skills and share knowledge
Flexibility in working hours when needed



What's in it for you:

This is an exciting opportunity for you who would like to work in a truly international business environment. If you enjoy being able to make things run smoothly even in rapidly changing situations, this is a chance for you to show your abilities while learning about the business. In this role, you work closely with the global Legal and Compliance team and other stakeholders.

We offer benefits, such as healthcare, Epass Flex, Auntie mental well-being service, flexible working times, buddy support, learning opportunities, and networking and social activities in all our locations throughout the summer.

The expected monthly salary for our full-time trainee roles is between 2130-2970 euros per month. The exact monthly salary for each role is based on the candidate's number of completed academic credits and job location.
