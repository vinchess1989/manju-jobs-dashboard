Täydellinen työnkuvaus

We are strengthening our specialist Commercial Due Diligence and Strategy team , advising private equity investors, selected corporate acquirers and portfolio company leadership teams.

Our core work is commercial due diligence, growth strategy and value creation across the investment lifecycle. For private equity clients, we support target screening, commercial due diligence, value creation planning, portfolio company strategy and exit preparation. For corporate clients, we focus on commercial due diligence and acquisition-related growth questions, working alongside Deloitte's M&A, tax, legal, technology and sector teams.

A distinctive strength is our integrated due diligence approach, combining commercial, product, technology and data perspectives to assess market attractiveness and business scalability. This is particularly relevant for technology, software, digital platforms, data-rich businesses, IT services and tech-enabled services.

Your Responsibilities

You will work on high-impact engagements delivering:

Commercial due diligence on acquisition targets, assessing market attractiveness, growth outlook, customer needs, competitive positioning, pricing, channel dynamics, risks and upside potential
Support across the investment lifecycle: target screening, value creation planning, portfolio company growth strategy and exit preparation
Integrated due diligence combining commercial and technology perspectives
Assessments on how commercial performance links to product, technology and operating model factors, such as product scalability, technology-enabled differentiation, data assets, customer stickiness, delivery model and platform readiness
Fact-based analysis through market sizing, customer and expert interviews, survey analysis, competitor benchmarking, financial analysis, product and business model assessment and secondary research
Clear translation of findings into investment and board-level implications, including market potential, growth levers, downside risks and value creation priorities
Senior client-ready materials: investment committee papers, board materials, executive presentations and management workshops

For a Consultant / Senior Consultant , the role will focus on owning workstreams, driving analysis, producing client-ready output and developing strong commercial and investor judgement.

For a Project Lead / Manager , the role will also include leading day-to-day delivery, structuring the workplans, story lining, managing client and partner interactions, guiding teams, ensuring quality and developing the next generation of consultants.

Why Deloitte?

At Deloitte, we value every individual and prioritise feedback and coaching at all stages of your career.
Our flat hierarchy means that work is centred around the strengths of the deloittees. We make a positive impact on each other, our clients, and society through our significant client projects.
We work with a hybrid model and aim to offer flexibility to our employees. We value face-to-face collaboration and learning at our comfortable office in Ruoholahti, while also providing opportunities for remote work.

Qualifications

Consultant / Senior Consultant

2–4 years in strategy consulting, commercial due diligence, transaction services, private equity, corporate strategy, M&A, investment banking or PE-backed businesses
Direct exposure to commercial due diligence, market studies, growth strategy or value creation
Strong analytical skills: market sizing, business modelling, financial interpretation and data triangulation
Ability to assess markets, customers, competitors and commercial risks in transaction settings
Ability to own workstreams independently and produce high-quality, client-ready output
Interest in technology, software, digital platforms, data-driven business models or tech-enabled services
Strong PowerPoint and Excel skills; Experience with surveys, expert interviews, Capital IQ, PitchBook, Factiva, Tableau, Alteryx or Python is a plus.
Strong written and verbal communication in English. Local language skills are an advantage where relevant.

Experience with technology due diligence, product strategy, software businesses, IT services, SaaS, digital platforms or tech-enabled value creation is an advantage.

Project Lead / Manager

4–6 years in top-tier strategy consulting, commercial due diligence, private equity, transaction advisory, corporate strategy or portfolio value creation
Proven track record in commercial due diligence, growth strategy or value creation, ideally with private equity or transaction-driven exposure
Ability to lead consulting teams in fast-paced diligence and strategy settings
Strong senior stakeholder skills, including the ability to work with private equity investment professionals, corporate development teams, CEOs, CFOs, commercial leaders and board-level audiences
Strong problem structuring, hypothesis generation, prioritization and synthesis
Commercial judgement, investor mindset and comfort working with ambiguity
Ability to work with technology specialists and translate technical findings into commercial and investment implications
Experience coaching consultants and analysts, providing clear feedback and maintaining quality under tight timelines

Experience in integrated commercial and technology diligence, software, digital platforms, data businesses, tech-enabled services or technology value creation is a strong advantage.
