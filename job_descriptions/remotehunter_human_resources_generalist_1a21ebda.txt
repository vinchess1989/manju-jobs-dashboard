1. About Our Client:

The organization operates emission testing programs tailored to local requirements in various states and counties across the US. It partners with government agencies to enhance compliance with air quality standards and support public policy objectives related to environmental quality.




2. About the Opportunity:

The Human Resources Generalist manages daily Human Resources functions including employee relations, leave administration, compliance, and benefits support. This role works closely with program leaders across multiple states to maintain consistent, fair, and compliant HR practices and foster a positive and inclusive work environment.




3. Responsibilities:

Manage HR functions across the employee life-cycle
Lead employee relations efforts including investigations, disciplinary actions, conflict resolution, and training
Partner with leadership and employees to ensure equitable workforce practices
Collaborate on training development and delivery
Monitor and document compliance with training requirements
Support benefits administration
Analyze employee data and company trends to provide actionable recommendations
Work with HR colleagues across multiple states
Contribute to HR department goals, objectives, and systems
Administer FMLA and other leave programs
Support workers' compensation claim administration
Ensure compliance with employment laws and recommend best practices
Conduct OSHA, EEO, and BLS reporting and assist with other regulatory reporting
Respond to unemployment claims and participate in hearings
Assist in developing safety initiatives
Travel up to 5% as needed
Perform other duties as assigned




4. Requirements:

3-5 years of Human Resources experience
Knowledge of federal and state employment laws
Familiarity with leave and workers' compensation programs
Proficiency in Microsoft Office
Ability to analyze data and develop solutions
Ability to pass background investigation and drug screening
Preferred: Bachelor's degree in Human Resources or related field
Preferred: Experience with multi-state employers
Preferred: Experience with benefits administration
Preferred: Knowledge of HRIS systems




5. Pay Range and Compensation Package:

$75,000 - $85,000 per year
