Type: Contractor/Part-Time

Compensation: $15 - $25/hour

Location: Remote

Commitment: 10-40 hrs/week




Role Responsibilities

Conduct legal research and analyze complex legal scenarios across multiple practice areas.
Review contracts, regulatory filings, and legal documentation for compliance and accuracy.
Interpret laws, regulations, and policies to support structured legal evaluations.
Prepare concise written summaries and legal reasoning for case-based scenarios.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to improve legal workflows and documentation standards.
Identify inconsistencies, legal risks, and compliance gaps within legal materials.




Requirements:

Have strong expertise in legal research, contract analysis, or regulatory interpretation.
Possess exceptional written communication and documentation skills.
Demonstrate high attention to detail and analytical reasoning abilities.
Be familiar with legal compliance standards and regulatory frameworks.
Have the ability to manage multiple priorities and deadlines in a remote environment.
