About The Company

Corteva Agriscience is a leading global agriculture company dedicated to advancing innovation, technology, and sustainable practices to address the world's food security challenges. Committed to building the future of agriculture, Corteva focuses on developing innovative solutions that empower farmers, improve crop yields, and promote environmental stewardship. With a strong emphasis on research and development, the company integrates science and technology to create products and services that make a meaningful difference in agriculture and society at large. Corteva's culture is rooted in collaboration, integrity, and a shared purpose to help grow what's next for a better tomorrow.

About The Role

We are seeking a highly organized and proactive Administrative Assistant to join our team in a fully remote capacity. In this role, you will be instrumental in supporting our leadership and departmental functions by managing schedules, coordinating meetings, and handling various administrative tasks. Your ability to prioritize effectively, communicate clearly, and maintain confidentiality will be essential to ensuring smooth operations across the organization. This position offers an excellent opportunity for candidates with strong administrative skills to contribute to a forward-thinking company that values innovation and diversity. As part of our team, you will help facilitate efficient workflows, support special projects, and foster positive relationships with internal teams and external partners.

Qualifications


High School Diploma or GED required
3-5 years of experience as an administrative professional preferred
Associate degree or equivalent experience accepted
Proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint, Word, Teams)
Strong interpersonal and communication skills, both written and verbal
Demonstrated problem-solving abilities and critical thinking skills
Ability to handle confidential information with discretion
Excellent time management and organizational skills
Experience with project management and supporting business initiatives is a plus
Ability to work independently and exercise sound judgment
Attention to detail and professionalism in all tasks


Responsibilities


Manage complex calendars, schedule meetings, and coordinate travel arrangements for internal and external stakeholders
Prepare meeting agendas, materials, and ensure all logistics are arranged in advance
Organize and facilitate in-person and virtual meetings, including distributing follow-up actions
Create, update, and maintain presentations, reports, and spreadsheets
Handle confidential files and records with discretion and professionalism
Assist with departmental budget tracking and project documentation to support organizational goals
Support special projects by gathering data, monitoring progress, and ensuring deadlines are met
Filter and prioritize requests, managing competing demands effectively for leadership attention
Build and maintain strong relationships with internal teams and external partners, representing leadership professionally
Process data entry, manage accounts receivable/payable, and support departmental operations

Parental leave with a minimum of 16 weeks for both mothers and fathers
Retirement savings plans to support future financial security
Tuition reimbursement programs to encourage continuous learning
Additional benefits and details available through our total rewards package
