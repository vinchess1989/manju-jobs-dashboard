What To Expect (Job Responsibilities)


Maintain calendars, track deadlines, route incoming calls, organize meetings, and prepare reports and presentations
Maintain document control procedures and ensure compliance
Make phone calls to gather or distribute information as needed
Populate boilerplate contracts for execution and issue purchase orders

Minimum educational requirement: Associate's Degree (preferred)
4+ years of experience in an office environment
2+ years of experience as an Administrative Assistant
Proficiency in both Microsoft Office and Google Suite
Ability to type 40+ words per minute


How To Stand Out (Preferred Qualifications)


Experience or familiarity with construction and/or real estate
Excellent organizational and problem-solving skills
Ability to manage multiple priorities effectively
Exceptional verbal and written communication skills
