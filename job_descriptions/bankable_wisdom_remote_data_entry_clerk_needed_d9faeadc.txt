Job Title: Remote Data Entry Clerk Needed at Kragelunde Efterskole

Kragelunde Efterskole is for young people who have difficulty reading and spelling.

We have 25 years of experience with our target group and have always followed both research and development of aids – typically within IT.

Tasks

This position will be responsible for processing a large amount of data and creating a company standard file naming convention, and filing system which enables the team to access information easily.

This position requires good computer skills and someone who likes and understands data management.

Responsibilities

Input latest information into database systems


Create and manage spreadsheets.
Regularly update existing database system records


Answering and following up on coworker inquiries by email/chat.


Resolves minor processing problems; seeks assistance from supervisor or other appropriate staff for issues beyond immediate expertise.
Maintains confidentiality of sensitive information.


Skills

Excellent computer skills


Experience with spreadsheets (MS Excel, Google Sheets)
Exceptional attention to detail
Strong data management and organizational Skills
