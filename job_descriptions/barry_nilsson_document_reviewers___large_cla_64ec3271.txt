About Us

What sets our firm apart is not just what we do, but who we are: a team known for its collaborative spirit and commitment to excellence, inclusion, and innovation. As an award-winning national law firm, we’ve built a culture that empowers you to not only grow your career but be your authentic self while you do it.

About The Team

Our national Insurance & Health practice combines deep industry knowledge with local expertise and the highest standards of client service. Our team has the depth of experience and agility to help our clients, both in Australia and overseas, to navigate the complex risk landscape and support their insureds. By joining us, you will become part of a welcoming and supportive team of highly skilled legal and administrative professionals who are passionate about what they do.

About The Role

We’re seeking multiple Document Reviewers to join our team to work on a large class action. The roles will focus on first level review, second level review and QA as part of the discovery phase of a large-scale class action. It’s anticipated that discovery will run for 3 – 6 months. This is a fantastic opportunity to gain hands-on experience on a large matter while working alongside a team of specialists in their field.

About You

If the following sounds like you, we would love to hear from you:


you have 2-4 years of prior document review experience, ideally in disputes or in response to regulatory notice
you have had experience reviewing high volumes of documents and are familiar with eDiscovery platforms like Relativity or Nuix
you have strong attention to detail


Please note that we are open to candidates in Adelaide, Brisbane, Melbourne, Perth or Sydney.
