Räätälöi ansioluetteloni
At Verda, we're building a fully featured European AI cloud — with everything needed to train, experiment with, and deploy AI models at scale.

We are a full-stack AI infrastructure company, meaning we design, operate, and optimize the compute powering modern AI workloads across training and inference. Our infrastructure runs on 100% renewable energy, helping build a more sustainable AI ecosystem.

Join Verda while it's still being built — not once it's finished.

About The Role

As Verda continues to grow internationally, we're looking for a Legal Intern to support our legal function across a broad range of commercial and operational matters.

This is a hands-on role for someone early in their legal career who wants exposure to the realities of building and scaling a technology company. You won't be sitting on the sidelines conducting theoretical legal research. Instead, you'll work closely with our Lead Legal Counsel and business teams to help keep deals moving, support internal stakeholders, and improve how legal operates across the company.

We're looking for someone who is pragmatic, commercially minded, and motivated by helping the business achieve outcomes efficiently while managing risk appropriately.

Your responsibilities


Support in reviewing, drafting, and negotiating a variety of commercial agreements, including non-disclosure agreements, customer, supplier, vendor, and partnership contracts
Support the legal review process for commercial opportunities and strategic initiatives
Conduct legal research and provide concise, practical recommendations to internal stakeholders
Assist with contract lifecycle management, record keeping, and legal operations processes
Help develop and maintain legal templates, playbooks, guidance materials, and internal policies
Support compliance, governance, corporate, and regulatory matters as required
Work closely with teams across Sales, Finance, Operations, Procurement, and People to provide timely legal support
Identify opportunities to improve legal processes and increase efficiency as the company scales
Help ensure legal risks are identified, communicated clearly, and managed in a commercially sensible way


Your key competencies


Recently completed a law degree or 3+ years of legal studies, or approximately 1 year of relevant experience in a law firm or an in-house legal team
Strong written and verbal communication skills in English
Excellent attention to detail and organisational skills
Ability to manage multiple priorities and work independently
Strong commercial awareness and interest in technology businesses
Ability to communicate legal concepts in a clear and practical way
Comfortable working in a fast-paced and evolving environment
Strong ownership mentality and willingness to take initiative


Most Importantly, You Are


Pragmatic
Results-oriented and business-minded
Focused on solutions rather than obstacles
Curious, proactive, and eager to learn
Comfortable operating with ambiguity and changing priorities


Nice to have


Experience working with commercial contracts
Exposure to technology, SaaS, cloud, AI, or infrastructure-related legal matters
Familiarity with GDPR, privacy, or compliance topics
Additional European language skills
Previous experience in a scale-up or venture-backed company
Comfortable using AI-powered tools to improve legal workflows and productivity


Why Verda


Opportunity to gain broad exposure across commercial, corporate, compliance, and operational legal matters
Direct mentorship from an experienced in-house legal leader
Fast learning environment with meaningful responsibility from day one
Work alongside ambitious teams building critical infrastructure for the AI ecosystem
International environment with colleagues from dozens of nationalities
Opportunity to contribute to a company scaling rapidly across multiple markets


Practicalities


Location: Helsinki
Hybrid mode: Full-time employees are expected to work from our Helsinki office 3 days per week. More flexibility is available for part-time arrangements.
Employment type: Full-time or part-time
Duration: Initial 6-month contract
Reporting line: Lead Legal Counsel
