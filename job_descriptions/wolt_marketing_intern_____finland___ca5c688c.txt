About Wolt

At Wolt, we create technology that brings joy, simplicity and earnings to the neighborhoods of the world. In 2014 we started with delivery of restaurant food. Now we’re building the delivery of (almost) everything and you’ll find us in over 500 cities in 30 countries around the world. In 2022 we joined forces with DoorDash and together we keep on dreaming big and expanding across the globe.

Working at Wolt isn’t always easy, but it’s definitely exciting. Here you’ll learn more, build more, and ship more than in most other companies. You’ll be challenged a lot, but also have a lot of fun on the way. So, if you’re a self-starter with drive and entrepreneurial spirit, this could be the ride of your life.

Location : Helsinki, Finland

Team : Local Marketing

Reporting to : Marketing Specialist / Marketing Manager

Duration : September–December 2026 (12 weeks)

Hours : 20 hours per week or as otherwise agreed

At Wolt, we're building the leading local commerce platform across Europe and Asia, helping people discover and get what they need, quickly and reliably.

Marketing plays a key role in how we connect with customers, support local merchants, and bring our brand to life across channels. From local campaigns and social media initiatives to content creation and influencer collaborations, we are constantly looking for new ways to engage audiences and create meaningful customer experiences.

As part of Wolt's Fall Semester Internship Program, we're looking for an ambitious final-year university student to join our Marketing team and gain hands-on experience working on real marketing initiatives with visible business impact.

About The Role

This is not a traditional internship focused on observation and administrative support.

As a Marketing Intern, you will support the execution of commercial and content marketing initiatives at the local level. Working closely with Marketing Specialists and Managers, you'll help bring campaigns to life by coordinating and executing tasks, preparing content, and assisting with operational and creative execution across multiple channels.

This role is ideal for a student interested in pursuing a career in marketing, communications, social media, or consumer technology.

What You'll Be Doing

Campaign Execution & Content Coordination


Assist in creating, localizing, and publishing marketing content across multiple channels, including in-app banners
Use internal tools to help set up campaigns, schedule content and upload marketing assets
Support the localization of global and regional marketing materials for the Finnish market
Track campaign setup tasks and checklists to ensure assets are ready and campaigns are delivered on time


Creative & Content Production


Collaborate with designers and content creators by preparing briefs, managing asset versions, and coordinating feedback
Help organize marketing assets and ensure content is accurate and ready for launch
Collect and organize user-generated content (UGC) for use across social media and campaign channels


Social Media & Influencer Marketing


Help schedule and publish content on local social media channels under the guidance of the Marketing Specialist or Manager
Assist with influencer outreach and campaign coordination
Organize product deliveries, collect campaign content and coordinate timelines for influencer collaborations


Cross-Functional Coordination


Assist in uploading and maintaining materials related to local merchant campaigns
Coordinate timelines between the marketing team and other internal stakeholders to support the smooth rollout of initiatives


What We're Looking For

Must Have


Currently in the final year of a Bachelor's or Master's degree
Strong interest in marketing, communications, social media, or consumer technology
Excellent organizational and coordination skills
Strong attention to detail and ability to manage multiple tasks simultaneously
Strong communication skills in English and Finnish
Proactive attitude and willingness to learn and take ownership


Nice to Have


Previous internship, student organization, or project experience in marketing, communications, business, or startups
Experience with social media platforms and content creation
Familiarity with project coordination and stakeholder management
Interest in technology, e-commerce, marketplaces, or consumer brands


What Success Looks Like

By the end of the internship, you will have:


Contributed to the successful delivery of multiple local marketing campaigns
Supported content creation and campaign execution across various marketing channels
Developed practical experience in campaign operations, social media and influencer marketing
Built strong stakeholder management and project coordination skills
Gained hands-on experience working in a fast-paced marketing environment


Why this role matters

This internship is designed to give talented students meaningful responsibility while helping Wolt identify future marketing talent.

You'll gain practical experience across campaign execution and measurement, social media, influencer and content marketing, as well as marketing operations, while working on projects that directly impact how Wolt grows in Finland.

High-performing interns may be considered for future full-time opportunities following graduation.

Why join Wolt?


Work on real projects with visible business impact
Learn from experienced marketing professionals
Gain hands-on experience across multiple areas of marketing
Collaborate with teams across Marketing, Creative, Commercial, and Operations
Build practical skills that accelerate your marketing career
Create a pathway toward future full-time opportunities at Wolt


Next steps

If you're excited about learning how modern marketing works, developing practical experience, and making an impact while you study, we'd love to hear from you.

After submitting your application, our Talent Acquisition team will review your profile. If it looks like a good fit, you'll go through a short interview process with the hiring manager and team. The process is designed to be transparent, efficient, and give you a real sense of what working at Wolt is like.

Please note that we do not accept applications sent by email, please submit your application through our careers website.
