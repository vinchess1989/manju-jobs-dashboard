Why Consider This Job Opportunity


Salary up to $45.00 per hour
Flexible remote work option available
Opportunity to work with the USDA Agricultural Research Service, contributing to important federal programs
Supportive environment focused on experienced workers (ages 55+)
Chance to utilize and enhance administrative and project management skills


What To Expect (Job Responsibilities)


Provide administrative support, project management, and training for the BARC Center Office
Review documents, analyze data, and maintain electronic files for program support
Assist with entering IAS Requisitions and tracking in the CATs system
Review OSQR CRIS projects, Annual Reports, and RPES Case Write-ups for scientists
Communicate with staff and coordinate training and meetings for Center leadership


What Is Required (Qualifications)


Minimum of 30 years of experience in administrative support and management, or a High School Diploma/GED
Expertise with all USDA-ARS systems
Experience with Windows, MS Word, MS Excel, and MS PowerPoint
Ability to perform duties without signing federal documents or making decisions on federally funded research
Must be legally eligible to work in the United States


How To Stand Out (Preferred Qualifications)


Previous experience working in federal agencies or with government programs
Familiarity with project management tools and techniques
Strong organizational skills and attention to detail
Excellent communication and interpersonal skills
Ability to work independently and as part of a team
