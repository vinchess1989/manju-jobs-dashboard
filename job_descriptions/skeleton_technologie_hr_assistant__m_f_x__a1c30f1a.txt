Skeleton Technologies is looking for a hands-on

HR Assistant (Summer Trainee) to support our HR operations at our Varkaus factory. This is a fixed-term summer position, running until mid-August.

The HR Assistant supports the HR team in daily HR operations and works closely with employees, line managers, and other support functions.

Key tasks:


Assisting with employment-related matters
Updating and maintaining employee data in HR systems
Maintaining HR documentation in systems and collecting payroll-related materials to be submitted to the outsourced payroll provider
Assisting in the recruitment process
Supporting onboarding arrangements for new employees and summer trainees


Requirements

Are you the one we are looking for?


Studies in Human Resources, Business Administration, or a related field OR some prior experience in HR-related tasks
Interest in practical, operational HR work
Good organizational skills
Fluent Finnish and English
Ability to handle confidential information with discretion


Why should you join?

Personal growth is guaranteed. You get to work in an international company that is tackling challenges from scale up of new factories, to development of new technologies, and sales to worldwide customers.

Skeleton has a clear purpose - we fight climate change by providing short duration energy storage technology.

Benefits

We're not just about energy storage. We're about redefining energy for a net zero future, and we'd love for you to be a part of our journey.

About Skeleton

Skeleton Technologies is a Global Cleantech 100 company and a technology leader in high-power energy storage for transportation, grid, automotive, and industrial applications.

For us, developing and producing the best energy storage solutions is a means to an end: helping companies reduce CO2 emissions and save energy.

We work with some of the largest companies in the world from automotive and industrial OEMs to truck fleet operators and aerospace prime contractors. Our products decrease CO2 emissions and fuel consumption, improve power quality, and empower wider electrification of the biggest industries globally.

Our supercapacitor and SuperBattery energy storage technologies, based on patented curved graphene, represent the biggest technological advancement in the industry in the last 20 years and the use of this innovative material provides our products with an unparalleled advantage in power and energy density.

Skeleton Technologies Group is represented in 7 different locations: current manufacturing in Großröhrsdorf, offices in Berlin and Leipzig Superfactory (Markranstädt), materials development in Bitterfeld-Wolfen, electrode production in Varkaus (Finland), R&D Lab in Helsinki, and engineering in Tallinn, Estonia. Within a few months, we will open the largest supercapacitor factory in the world in Markranstädt, Germany.
