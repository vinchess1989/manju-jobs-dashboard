Company Description Poplar Events LLC is a full-service, hands-on event planning and management company specializing in memorable in-person, virtual, and hybrid events. The team brings years of experience to each client engagement, handling all aspects of planning and execution from concept through post-event wrap-up. Poplar Events LLC partners with highly acclaimed companies across the tri-state region, coordinating production, catering, venues, and vendors. The company focuses on making complex events feel seamless so clients can focus on shining in front of their audiences. Team members join a collaborative environment that values professionalism, creativity, and client satisfaction.




Role Description This is a full-time, remote Event Coordinator role. The Event Coordinator will support the end-to-end planning and execution of events, including timelines, budgets, vendor outreach, and logistics. Responsibilities include coordinating with clients to understand goals and requirements, preparing proposals and event briefs, and maintaining detailed project plans. The role involves sourcing and liaising with venues and vendors, tracking contracts and invoices, and ensuring deliverables meet quality standards and deadlines. The Event Coordinator will assist with pre-event rehearsals, manage day-of-event communications and issue resolution, and support post-event reporting and feedback collection.




Qualifications

Event Planning and Event Management skills to coordinate timelines, logistics, budgets, and vendor relationships from concept through post-event follow-up.
Strong Communication and Customer Service skills to build trust with clients, manage expectations, and resolve issues in a professional, responsive manner.
Sales and relationship-building skills to support proposals, upsell event services, and contribute to business growth opportunities.
Excellent organizational, time-management, and multitasking abilities with strong attention to detail in a fast-paced, deadline-driven environment.
Proficiency with common office and collaboration tools (e.g., Google Workspace or Microsoft 365, virtual meeting platforms, project management software).
Ability to work independently in a remote setting while collaborating effectively with a distributed team.
Prior experience in event coordination, hospitality, marketing, or a related field is preferred; relevant education or certifications in event management are a plus.
