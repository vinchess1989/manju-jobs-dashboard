THIS IS US

Dott is the European champion of shared micro-mobility. Created through the merger of operators TIER and Dott in March 2024, the company decided to move forward under the name Dott and to integrate all vehicles into the Dott app. With the mission to change mobility for good, the team is led by CEO Henri Moissinac and President Maxim Romain. TIER-Dott facilitates sustainable travel, reduces congestion and pollution in cities, and decreases reliance on cars. With more than 250,000 shared vehicles in more than 400 cities across 21 countries in Europe and the Middle East, the 10 million users have generated over 400 million rides so far. For more information, visit www.ridedott.com

THE ROLE AND YOUR IMPACT AT DOTT

Our team is building the foundation for how we work with 3PL partners — designing cooperation frameworks, setting standards, monitoring compliance, and continuously improving processes across the full partner lifecycle. This role sits at the centre of that work, supporting team members across a range of projects as we scale and mature our partner operations.

You'll be juggling multiple workstreams at once, so comfort with shifting priorities and an ability to stay organised across concurrent tasks is just as important as any technical skill. You will join a team of 2 Partner Operations Coordinators and report directly to the Head of Operations.

What You'll Do


Write and maintain process maps, SOPs, and operational documentation — making best practices clear and scalable
Use tools like Google Sheets, AI assistants, and automation platforms to build lightweight workflows and support reporting
Support team members across project tasks — from compliance tracking to framework development — adapting to shifting priorities as the team's needs evolve
Implement and embed the company's operational best practices across the 3PL partner lifecycle


Experience

YOU’LL FIT GREAT WITH THESE SKILLS AND QUALIFICATIONS

Recent graduate with up to 1 year of professional experience — we're looking for someone at the start of their career who's eager to learn and grow

Skills


Comfortable with data and analysis — not a heavy analyst, but able to write a query, build a tracker, and spot what the numbers are saying
Proficient in Google Sheets or Excel — formulas, pivot tables, and basic data manipulation
Able to design and document process flows — writing SOPs and procedures others can follow
Curious about AI and automation tools — comfortable using them to support workflows, even without a technical background
Able to manage multiple priorities at once — organised and focused even when workstreams shift or overlap
Structured, proactive, and calm under pressure — you plan ahead and don't get overwhelmed easily
Able to work with basic guidelines and take a task to completion — understanding what's needed and delivering without requiring constant direction


Languages


English required
French or Italian is a strong plus
