HR and People Operations Professionals – Paid Research Study

About Prolific

Prolific is not just another player in the AI space – we are building the biggest pool of quality human data in the world.

Over 35,000 AI developers, researchers, and organizations use Prolific to gather data from paid study participants with a wide variety of experiences, knowledge, and skills.

The role

We are looking for HR professionals to participate in paid research studies and surveys. This opportunity is particularly relevant for HR Managers, HR Specialists, Talent Acquisition professionals, People Operations professionals, and other HR practitioners. Researchers are interested in understanding how HR teams use workflow management, collaboration, and workplace technology tools to manage processes, projects, employee operations, and business activities.

Participants who qualify for these studies may receive payments of up to $200 per study, depending on the project requirements and time commitment.

What you'll bring


Professional experience in Human Resources, Talent Acquisition, or People Operations
Experience working with project, workflow, collaboration, or task management tools
Strong communication skills
Ability to follow research instructions carefully
Fluent English skills


What you'll be doing in the role


Participating in research studies and surveys focused on workplace tools and business processes
Sharing feedback on workflow management, project management, and collaboration tools
Providing insights into how HR teams manage projects, processes, and operational workflows
Helping researchers better understand how professionals evaluate and adopt workplace technology
Contributing professional perspectives that support the development of future workplace tools and AI systems

Why Prolific is a great platform to join as a Participant

Joining our platform as a Prolific participant will give you the opportunity to contribute to research that helps shape the future of workplace tools, technology, and AI. By sharing your professional experience and expertise, you can help researchers and organizations better understand how professionals work, collaborate, and make decisions. Once you pass our assessment, you can join Prolific in just 15 minutes and start enjoying competitive pay rates, flexible hours, and the ability to work remotely.

We've built a unique platform that connects researchers and companies with a global pool of participants, enabling the collection of high-quality, ethically sourced human behavioural data and feedback. This data is the cornerstone of developing more accurate, nuanced, and aligned AI systems.

We believe that the next leap in AI capabilities won't come solely from scaling existing models, but from integrating diverse human perspectives and behaviours into AI development. By providing this crucial human data infrastructure, Prolific is positioning itself at the forefront of the next wave of AI innovation – one that reflects the breadth and the best of humanity.
