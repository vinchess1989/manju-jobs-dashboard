Legal Technology Expert

Contract | Remote

Pay: $55-$75 per hour

Time Commitment

Role Snapshot

Seeking legal operations and technology professionals to build realistic legal workflows and enterprise environments used to evaluate advanced language models across legal operations, contract management, and e-discovery functions.

Core Responsibilities

Build realistic digital workspaces reflecting day-to-day legal operations workflows and documentation

Design multi-step tasks involving legal intake, contract lifecycle management, e-discovery, matter management, and spend operations

Create structured scenarios requiring navigation across multiple platforms, files, and stakeholders

Collaborate with legal operations and technology experts to refine workflow realism, task scope, and evaluation rigor

Work asynchronously with research teams to improve benchmark design and legal-tech task quality

Translate complex legal operations processes into structured evaluation specifications

Ideal Candidate Profile

Strong experience in legal operations, legal technology, e-discovery, contract lifecycle management, or matter management

Background in legal-spend management, legal intake, knowledge management, or enterprise legal program design

Familiarity with platforms such as Relativity, Icertis, HotDocs, Contract Express, Clio, or MyCase

CLOC or ACC Legal Ops involvement is a plus

Strong analytical writing and ability to structure legal workflows into detailed task specifications

Easy Apply to proceed.
