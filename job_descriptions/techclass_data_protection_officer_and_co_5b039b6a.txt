About TechClass 

TechClass is a leading eLearning provider, dedicated to delivering high-quality, engaging, and accessible educational content to learners worldwide. Our mission is to empower individuals and organizations to achieve their learning and development goals through our innovative and user-friendly platform.




Job Overview 

We are looking for a Data Protection Officer and Compliance Training Specialist. In this role you will be responsible for overseeing TechClass’s data protection operations while simultaneously acting as the on-camera Compliance related specialist for our interactive compliance courses. You will ensure our organization remains fully compliant with data protection laws and use your expertise to translate complex legal jargon into engaging, easy-to-understand educational videos.




Key Responsibilities

Regulatory Compliance: Oversee and manage our data protection strategy, ensuring full compliance with GDPR and relevant data privacy laws.
Data Management: Handle internal data processing audits, manage data subject requests, and ensure vendor contract compliance.
Course Evaluation: Review and evaluate our existing and upcoming compliance course materials to ensure total legal accuracy and relevance.
On-Camera Instruction: Confidently and naturally appear on camera as the expert instructor for our interactive corporate training videos.
Simplifying Concepts: Transform dry, dense legal guidelines into pedagogical, engaging, and highly relatable content for a global audience.




Requirements

Privacy Expertise: Proven professional experience as a Data Protection Officer or IT security/compliance expert with a deep understanding of GDPR.
On-Camera Presence: Comfortable in front of a lens, with the ability to maintain high energy and professionalism when delivering technical information.
English Fluency: High proficiency in English. You must have excellent articulation, clear pronunciation, and the ability to teach confidently in English.
