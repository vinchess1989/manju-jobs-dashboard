What is this role all about?

We are looking for a proactive and hands-on People Advisor (HR Generalist) to join Epical on a 12-month fixed-term contract while one of our colleagues is on parental leave.

In this role, you will act as a trusted partner to managers and employees across both Finland and Sweden, supporting both day-to-day HR operations and more complex people-related topics.

This is a broad HR Generalist role where you’ll combine operational excellence with advisory responsibilities, from employee lifecycle management and compensation & benefits to employment law and change processes.

You will play an important role in ensuring smooth HR delivery while contributing to a positive employee experience and a strong, trust-based culture.

Key responsibilities include:


Acting as the main HR partner for Finland, while supporting HR matters in Sweden when needed
Advising on employment law, collective agreements, and employee relations topics, including performance management, work ability, and change-related situations
Managing compensation & benefits processes
Handling HR operations across the employee lifecycle (contracts, onboarding, offboarding, HR data)
Driving and supporting HR projects and process improvements
Acting as Work Safety Manager and leading the Occupational Health & Safety committee
Acting as back-up for payroll in Finland


Who are we looking for?

We’re looking for someone who combines structure with a “doer” mindset, a person who enjoys rolling up their sleeves, building relationships, and making an impact through HR.

To succeed in this role, you likely have:


3+ years of experience working in HR
A university degree in HR, Business Administration, Law, Psychology, or similar
Strong knowledge of Finnish employment law (Swedish employment law is a plus)
Experience working with compensation & benefits
Fluency in Finnish, Swedish, and English (spoken and written)
Experience supporting and advising managers


We also believe you are:


A strong relationship builder who quickly earns trust
A collaborative team player who contributes to a positive team dynamic
Structured and reliable, with the ability to manage multiple priorities
Proactive and solution-oriented, you take initiative and get things done
Curious and confident, you dare to challenge and improve ways of working
A person of high integrity, handling sensitive topics with discretion


Experience from data consultancy and working with the Swedish market is a plus.

Why join Epical?

At Epical, trust and collaboration are at the heart of everything we do. Here’s what you can expect as a part of our team:


Work-life balance: Flexible remote working options and an environment that prioritizes your well-being.
Stimulating assignments: Work on exciting projects with large enterprise clients in complex environments.
Comprehensive benefits: Enjoy a competitive compensation package, training opportunities, and certifications to support your success.
A mission-driven organization: Be part of a company dedicated to creating a fairer world by leveraging digital identity responsibly.


Practical information:


Location: Espoo, Finland
Start: Preferably mid August
Duration: at least 12 months (Parental leave cover)


To qualify, you must reside in Finland and have a valid work permit. Please note that background and credit checks may be conducted.
