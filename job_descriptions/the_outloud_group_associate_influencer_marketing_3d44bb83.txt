ASSOCIATE INFLUENCER MARKETING MANAGER – STRATEGY




The Associate Influencer Marketing Manager plays a key role in building and executing thoughtful, high-impact influencer marketing strategies. You’ll help shape campaign direction, develop creative and data-informed approaches, and support brands as they grow their presence across YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and podcasts.




You’ll own portions of brand strategy, contribute meaningfully to campaign planning, and actively participate in client conversations while continuing to build confidence as a strategic partner. You’ll work closely with senior team members, using data, platform insights, and industry trends to optimize influencer programs and deliver strong, measurable results.

This is a client-facing role for someone ready to take the next step in their influencer marketing career. You’re comfortable making recommendations, eager to grow your strategic voice, and excited to deepen your understanding of the creator economy.




WHAT YOU BRING TO THE TABLE




4+ years of experience in influencer marketing, digital marketing, or media partnerships, with hands-on experience contributing to campaign strategy and execution
Strong communication skills in English, with the ability to collaborate with clients, influencers, and internal teams
Solid understanding of influencer marketing best practices and platforms, including YouTube, Instagram, TikTok, and Podcast
Experience using performance data to inform decisions, identify trends, and improve campaign outcomes
Growing confidence in client-facing situations, including contributing to strategy discussions and recommendations
Creative and critical thinking skills to identify opportunities for brand growth within the influencer space
Proficiency in tools such as Google Workspace, Microsoft Office, and project management software (e.g., Trello)
Ability to work independently and collaboratively within a remote, full-time role based in the United States
Ability to travel occasionally for client work, team events (offsites, meet-ups), and industry conferences as needed
