Your mission

Would you like to work and develop your skills at an international company that truly values its people? As a Legal Trainee you will support the Hiab Legal and Compliance team.

The traineeship lasts for 12 months and is located in Helsinki. We expect the trainee to start working in September 2026. This position is part-time (2–3 days a week) from September until May, and full-time for the summer months of June-August.

Key Responsibilities

Your work will cover several fields of law ranging from international contract law and company law to IP and privacy law. Your responsibilities will include various tasks such as assisting Legal Counsels in contract drafting, taking care of trademark issues, researching legal information, and archiving legal material.

What We Are Looking For

We expect you to be a third or fourth year law student and have experience in working both independently and in a highly collaborative environment. We value a can-do attitude and a proactive approach to both complex issues as to very routine work. If you have experience in a similar position or in a law firm, that would be seen as an asset. As we work mainly in English, strong communication skills and fluency in both written and spoken English are required in this position.

What We Offer

We offer you a great possibility to develop your professional skills and get hands-on experience working with challenging tasks in a global company. You will have the possibility to get to know Hiab’s business and learn about the role of in-house lawyers. You will also work closely with the General Counsel which gives you the possibility to experience the daily tasks of corporate level in the listed company.

We embrace modern ways of working; in this role, you will get to work closely with advanced tools, utilize them in your daily tasks, and contribute to how we evolve our practices. After this trainee program you will have a good overall image of in-house lawyer’s work and career opportunities in an international company.

Ready to apply?
