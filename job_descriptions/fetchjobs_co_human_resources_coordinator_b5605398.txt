About The Company

Sysco is a global leader in the foodservice industry, dedicated to providing high-quality food products and related services to a diverse customer base, including restaurants, healthcare, educational institutions, and hospitality businesses. With a strong commitment to innovation, sustainability, and customer satisfaction, Sysco has established itself as a trusted partner in the food supply chain. The company prides itself on fostering a collaborative and inclusive work environment that encourages growth, development, and excellence among its employees.

About The Role

This Human Resources (HR) role at Sysco is designed to support the daily operations of the HR department by providing essential administrative assistance. The position involves executing regional and corporate HR programs, facilitating effective communication, and maintaining compliance with employment laws and company policies. The successful candidate will play a vital role in creating an engaging and inclusive workplace environment that attracts, develops, and retains talent aligned with Sysco’s strategic business objectives. This role offers an opportunity to contribute to a dynamic team, support employee engagement initiatives, and help drive organizational success through effective HR practices.

Qualifications

Educational qualifications for this role include a high school diploma; however, a college degree or certification in Human Resources is preferred. Candidates should have 2-3 years of experience in Human Resources, specifically in generalist, recruitment, or employee relations roles, or an equivalent combination of education and experience. Professional skills required include strong organizational abilities, analytical skills for data interpretation, and excellent communication skills. Knowledge of federal and state employment laws, proficiency in Microsoft Office applications, and the ability to manage multiple tasks efficiently are essential. The candidate must demonstrate a high level of professionalism, confidentiality, and the ability to work independently in a fast-paced environment.

Responsibilities


Execute core HR processes such as onboarding, orientation, exit interviews, and leave administration to ensure smooth employee transitions and compliance with company policies.
Support hiring managers and Talent Acquisition teams in recruitment efforts, including candidate screening and interview coordination.
Assist Human Resource Business Partners (HRBPs) in fostering an inclusive and diverse workplace culture.
Administer employee engagement and satisfaction programs to promote a positive work environment.
Provide technical support and customer service for major HR initiatives, projects, and systems.
Maintain and monitor compliance programs, ensuring adherence to legal and regulatory requirements.
Create, distribute, and manage communications such as memos, emails, presentations, and surveys to facilitate information flow across the organization.
Track data, consolidate responses, and generate reports to support decision-making and process improvements.
Provide administrative support for meetings, webinars, conference calls, and new hire orientations, ensuring smooth execution of HR-related events.
Perform additional duties as assigned to support the HR department’s objectives and operational needs.


Benefits

Sysco offers a comprehensive benefits package designed to support employees' health, well-being, and professional growth. This includes competitive health insurance plans, dental and vision coverage, retirement savings plans, paid time off, and employee assistance programs. The company also provides opportunities for ongoing training and development, career advancement, and a collaborative work environment that values diversity and inclusion. Additionally, employees are eligible for a sign-on bonus of $1,500, reflecting Sysco’s commitment to attracting top talent and rewarding dedication.
