Front Desk Receptionist (Winter Season 2026–2027)

About Halo Igloos

Halo Igloos is a premium resort in Rovaniemi, combining modern Nordic design, private wellness (sauna & jacuzzi) and authentic Arctic nature. We create high-end, immersive experiences for international guests looking to connect with Lapland in a unique and meaningful way.

We are now looking for service-minded Receptionists to join our team for the winter season 2026–2027.

About The Role

As a Front Desk Receptionist at Halo Igloos, you are the first and last point of contact for our guests.

You play a key role in shaping the overall experience by creating a warm, professional and seamless arrival and departure. From check-in to check-out, you ensure every interaction reflects our premium standards and attention to detail.

What You Will Do


Welcome guests and manage check-in and check-out processes
Handle reservations, payments and guest information
Provide information about the resort, services and local experiences
Respond to guest requests and resolve issues in a professional manner
Coordinate with housekeeping, maintenance and other departments
Assist with bookings, transfers and activity arrangements
Upsell additional services and enhance the guest experience
Maintain accurate records and ensure smooth front desk operations


What We Expect


Previous experience in reception, hospitality or customer service
Previous experience of MEWS, Fareharbor systems is a plus
Strong communication skills in English (other languages are a plus)
Professional, positive and service-oriented attitude
Ability to multitask and work in a fast-paced environment
Good organizational and problem-solving skills
Flexibility to work shifts, including evenings, weekends and holidays


What We Offer


Monthly salary 2400-2700€ + supplements (based on experience)
Flexible employment (full-time, part-time or on-call)
Opportunity to work in a premium international resort
Supportive and ambitious team
Assistance with accommodation
Training and onboarding
Opportunity to be part of building a growing international brand


Working Period

Winter season 2026–2027 (approximately November–March/April), could be also earlier on hourly basis
