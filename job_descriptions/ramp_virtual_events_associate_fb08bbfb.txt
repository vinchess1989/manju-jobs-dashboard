About Ramp

Ramp is building the smart infrastructure for finance teams, embedded in the transaction flow of every dollar a business spends. We automate how over $200B in annualized spend flows in and out of 70,000+ companies: authorizing payments, flagging risk, categorizing spend, and closing books.

The problems are high-stakes, data-dense, and unforgiving.

We hire people with high agency and high urgency. We look for slope over intercept. We care less about where you trained and more about what you’ve built. At Ramp, everyone is a builder who owns problems end to end and makes consequential decisions that shape the outcome.

The median Ramp customer saves 5% and grows revenue 16% in their first year – far in excess of businesses operating without Ramp. We believe every ambitious company deserves the same.

If you want to build systems that directly shape how companies move and manage billions, Ramp is the place to do it.

About The Role

The role will be a key part in bringing Ramp’s virtual experiences to life. In this position, you’ll help plan and execute engaging webinars, workshops and online events that generate leads, connect with customers, and elevate Ramp’s brand. You’ll collaborate across Marketing, Sales, and external partners to ensure every detail, from promotion to production, runs seamlessly. This role is ideal for someone who is highly organized, creative, and excited to grow within a fast-moving marketing team.

What You'll Do


Plan and execute original, engaging virtual events that drive lead generation
Coordinate platform setup, speaker prep, design materials, the live event, and post-event enablement
Manage promotional efforts across email, social media, and partners’ channels to boost event registration and attendance
Collaborate with internal teams and speakers to collect and organize event content and materials
Monitor event performance metrics to influence future campaigns
Partner with external vendors and partner teams to ensure high-quality virtual events
Support post-event initiatives, including content repurposing, lead follow-up, reporting, and sales enablement


What You Need


Experience in demand generation marketing, field or event management at startups or fast-growing tech or SaaS, with the ability to independently manage programs that result in high-impact events.
Ability to plan, support, and or execute up to double-digit virtual events per month
Proven track record of using project management tools such as Notion or Asana to stay organized and attentive to details
Experience with virtual events technology and complementary tools and platforms (e.g. Salesforce, Goldcast, Hubspot, On24, GTW, or similar CRM/marketing automations)
Excellent written and verbal communication skills with a customer service mindset for working with vendors and internal teams
Comfortable with design tools like Canva or Figma for creating virtual events promotional collateral
Deliberate about using AI to improve workflows, solve problems, and deliver more


Nice-to-Haves


Bachelor's degree in Marketing and/or related field
Experience working at fintech or finance company
Experience in high-growth startup environments
Exceptional at writing and designing for virtual events
Experience working cross-functionally with direct and indirect (channel) sales organizations
