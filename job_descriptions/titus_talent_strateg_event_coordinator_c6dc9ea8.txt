Event Coordinator - Performance Profile

The Event Coordinator will focus on coordinating and executing successful live and digital events that align with Titus’ strategic goals. This role will ensure seamless event logistics and execution while maintaining high-quality experiences for attendees. The Event Coordinator will play a key role in planning with excellence, strengthening relationships, and driving results through well-organized events.







Event Project Management

A major objective for the Event Coordinator is to oversee and manage all aspects of event projects from inception to completion.

Follow and enhance event planning procedures, timelines, and checklists.
Collaborate with key stakeholders to ensure event goals align with business objectives.
Track deliverables to ensure timely execution of tasks.
Ensure all events are executed within budget while maintaining high quality.
Continuously assess and refine event processes to improve efficiency and impact.
Report event success metrics, identifying areas for improvement.




Vendor Collaboration

This position will involve managing relationships with vendors to enhance event experiences.

Establish and maintain relationships with key event vendors.
Ensure vendors meet quality and budget expectations.
Coordinate with internal and external stakeholders to source, procure, and track company swag and event inventory.




Internal Coordination & Communication

The Event Coordinator will serve as a critical internal resource for all event-related activities.

Create and manage landing pages in HubSpot and WordPress.
Be the primary point of contact for internal teams regarding event logistics and updates.
Maintain accurate records of event planning and execution details in HubSpot and Asana.
Provide regular reports and insights (marketing) on event success and impact.
Gather feedback and data to improve future events.
Participate in internal team meetings and cross-functional collaborations.




Event Marketing & Promotion

The Event Coordinator will ensure successful event marketing to drive engagement and attendance.

Coordinate event marketing strategies, including email campaigns, social media, and promotional materials.
Work closely with the marketing team to create compelling content and visuals for events.
Ensure branding consistency across all event materials.
Maintain an event marketing checklist.
Track event registration and engagement metrics to optimize marketing efforts.




Event Coordinator Top Measurables

Event Project Management: Successfully execute a minimum of 3 events per quarter, ensuring all projects stay on schedule and within budget.
Vendor Collaboration: Maintain strong working relationships with internal and external vendors
Internal Coordination & Communication: Ensure event details are communicated effectively, with all internal stakeholders receiving necessary updates
Event Marketing & Promotion: Ensure all marketing materials are delivered on time and accurately track basic engagement metrics







Location: This is a mobile work opportunity. Ability and willingness to travel to 3-5 events a year.
