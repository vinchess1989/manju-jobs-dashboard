Job description

We are now looking for an Advanced Analytics Specialist to join our Decision Support and Advanced Analytics team in UPM Fibres. This position is primarily located in Helsinki, Finland. Other UPM locations can be considered.

Greetings from your future manager 

“Our team works at the intersection of technology and strategy, supporting impactful decisions across UPM Fibres. This is an opportunity to take part in accelerating our capabilities of utilizing analytics to help the business thrive.” – Jani Strandberg, Manager, Fibres Decision Support & Advanced Analytics 

What you will do 

In this role, you will develop analytics, optimization, and AI-enabled decision-support solutions to address business challenges across UPM Fibres. You will collaborate with experts and decision-makers to turn complex business questions into practical analytical solutions. 

Your main tasks are: 

Identify and shape analytics opportunities together with experts and decision-makers in UPM Fibres’ businesses 

Contribute to solving business challenges in areas such as production planning, logistics, supply chains, commercial operations, and strategy 

Take ownership of analytics workstreams or selected project responsibilities as part of collaborative project teams 

Drive analytics work from problem definition and methodology selection to analysis, modelling, conclusions, and implementation support 

Build and apply analytical models, such as optimization models, simulations, forecasting models, statistical models, and machine learning methods 

Participate in projects where generative AI, large language models, or AI agents are used to improve workflows, automate knowledge work, or support decision-making 

Engage with stakeholders to understand business needs, clarify assumptions, validate results, and turn analysis into practical recommendations 

Support the team’s competence development in areas such as optimization, data science, AI tools, and modern analytics practices 

Who you are 

You have a university degree, preferably M.Sc. or Ph.D., in mathematics, economics, computer science, engineering, operations research, statistics, or a related field 

You are fluent in English 

You have at least 2 years of experience in analytics, data science, optimization, operations research, or decision-support work 

You have practical programming experience in Python 

You have applied analytical methods to real-world business challenges, such as optimization, simulation, forecasting, statistical modelling or machine learning 

You have practical experience using generative AI or large language models in analytical, programming, or knowledge-work tasks; experience developing AI-assisted tools, agent workflows, or automation solutions is considered an advantage 

You communicate effectively with stakeholders to clarify business needs, communicate findings, and refine solutions based on feedback 

You take ownership of your contributions within projects while collaborating closely with colleagues, stakeholders, and subject-matter experts  

Experience in industrial, logistics, supply chain, production, forestry, pulp, timber, energy, or other asset-intensive business environments is considered an advantage 
  

This is what we offer to you 

A meaningful job: We have an inspiring purpose ‘We create a future beyond fossils.’ Our work is impactful and transformative. 

A caring community: Our community is built on great team spirit and shared commitment. We care about each other and the well-being of our colleagues. We support the work-life balance of all our employees. 

Development opportunities: We offer you great opportunities to learn and grow throughout your career. We are an international, multi-business organization offering plenty of avenues for personal growth. 

Responsible employer: Sustainability is at the core of everything we do. We are committed to ambitious, science-based sustainability targets in all areas from climate change to biodiversity. We foster diversity and inclusivity, offering a work environment in which everyone can be themselves. 

Learn about our Rewarding and our Ways of working. 

Additional information 

For further information about the role, please contact Jani Strandberg, Manager, Fibres Decision Support & Advanced Analytics, at jani.strandberg@upm.com or tel. +358407462665 on June 18 or June 26 at 10–11 a.m. EEST. 

To apply, please submit your CV and cover letter by June 28, 2026. Please note that we accept only applications submitted through our online application system. We begin reviewing applications as they are received, and you may be contacted before the application deadline. 

For support with submitting your application, please contact the HR Service Center at careers@upm.com or tel. +358204146600. 

A security background check may be performed for those selected to UPM positions, in accordance with the company recruitment guidelines and the Security Clearance Act 726/2014. 

About UPM 

UPM is a material solutions company, renewing products and entire value chains with an extensive portfolio of renewable fibres, advanced materials, decarbonization solutions, and communication papers. Our performance in sustainability has been recognized by third parties, including EcoVadis and the Dow Jones Sustainability Indices. We operate globally and employ approximately 15,800 people worldwide, with annual sales of approximately €10.3 billion. Our shares are listed on Nasdaq Helsinki Ltd.
