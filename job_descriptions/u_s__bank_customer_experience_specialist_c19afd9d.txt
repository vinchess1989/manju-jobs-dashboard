Job Summary

Join the College of Health Sciences as an Administrative Services Assistant supporting the Office of Research and Scholarship and the BioMotion Laboratory. You’ll be at the heart of a collaborative team, overseeing the daily operations that keep our research operation moving—scheduling, business orders, and select business office functions—while partnering directly with faculty and staff. This role is a great fit for someone who is well organized, confident working with computers, and excited by an environment where something new is always happening. We’re looking for a dedicated, hardworking person who values being part of something meaningful and takes ownership of their work.
