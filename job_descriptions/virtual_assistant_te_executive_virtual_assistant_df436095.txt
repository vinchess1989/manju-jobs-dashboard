Executive Virtual Assistant

Executive Virtual Assistant

Full-time

AEST (Australian Eastern Standard Time)

Are you a dynamic professional with a knack for managing the intricacies of business operations?

If so, we’re looking for you!

We are seeking a highly organized and adaptable Executive Virtual Assistant to join the team. In this role, you will play a pivotal part in managing the overall business operations, ensuring smooth workflows, and providing essential support to the team.

Responsibilities


Manage the overall business operations, including scheduling, correspondence, and task prioritization.
Review and approve content to ensure it aligns with our brand voice and standards.
Provide oversight on finance-related tasks, such as budget tracking and expense management.
Handle reception tasks, including answering calls, responding to emails, and greeting visitors.
Assist with website edits and updates, maintaining a user-friendly online presence.
Adapt quickly to new tasks and responsibilities as the needs of the business evolve.


Skills Required


Proven experience as an Executive Assistant or similar role, preferably in a virtual setting.
Exceptional organizational skills and attention to detail.
Strong communication and interpersonal abilities.
Proficiency in multitasking and prioritizing tasks effectively.
Familiarity with basic finance principles and content review processes.
Willingness to learn and adapt to new technologies and tasks.
Experience working in the health/ psychology industry is a plus
