Are you a hands-on Marketing Technology Manager passionate about delivering impactful customer engagement through data, technology, and personalized journeys? Do you enjoy implementing Martech solutions, collaborating with teams, and turning plans into practical execution? If this sounds like you, you might be the Marketing Technology Manager we’re looking for!

We Luxidians are a team of creative thinkers, strategists, data analysts, writers, designers, filmmakers, virtual world builders, developers and technologists. We use insight-led creativity to accelerate growth for our clients. With 30+ years of experience in delivering marketing and commerce solutions to a strong international client base, we help organizations break silos to build customer journeys that drive sales and support buyers.  

As a Marketing Technology Manager, you contribute to the implementation and development of marketing technology solutions that support business objectives and enhance the customer life cycle. You work closely with senior colleagues and cross-functional teams to ensure high-quality execution of Martech initiatives, combining hands-on delivery with structured planning and continuous improvement.

Main Responsibilities

Martech Execution & Development 


Implement Martech solutions based on defined strategies and business needs
Contribute to building and maintaining Martech roadmaps and development initiatives
Support the optimization of Martech platforms and processes
Identify improvement opportunities and raise development ideas


Campaign Enablement & Marketing Automation 


Execute customer segmentation, journeys, and personalization activities
Build and optimize marketing automation workflows and campaigns
Ensure quality and consistency in campaign execution
Support testing, reporting, and continuous improvement


Platform Operations 


Support day-to-day operations of Martech platforms (e.g. Salesforce or similar tools)
Ensure data accuracy and proper use of systems
Assist in integrations and platform improvements with internal stakeholders
Monitor performance and contribute to optimization actions


Collaboration & Project Contribution 


Work as part of project teams delivering Martech solutions
Collaborate with marketing, sales, product, and data teams
Contribute to knowledge sharing and best practices
Optionally support smaller workstreams or coordination tasks


Qualifications That Ensure Success


~3–5 years of experience in Martech, CRM, or marketing automation roles
Hands-on experience with marketing automation platforms (e.g. Salesforce or similar)
Understanding of customer data, segmentation, and campaign execution
Ability to execute and support development of Martech solutions
Strong organizational and problem-solving skills
Experience working in project environments
Fluent English (Finnish is considered an advantage)


What We Offer 


A supportive, highly skilled and experienced team of Martech professionals: At Luxid, everyone is encouraged to be themselves and bring their unique strengths to the table. Our daily work is built on close collaboration, open communication, and shared successes. We hold regular team meetings to share expertise and support each other. We also enjoy after work events, and team days – we want everyone to feel part of the group.  
Welcoming new team members: New employees are warmly welcomed, and they immediately get involved in projects and receive support from experienced team members. We have a clear onboarding program to help new colleagues quickly get to know Luxid’s ways of working and our culture. Every new employee has the opportunity to influence and develop their own role – ideas and initiatives are always valued!  
Opportunities for growth: We offer the chance to work with big international brands in trailblazing projects, earn industry certifications, and develop skills in diverse ways. We encourage participation in industry events, training, and conferences – continuous learning is part of our everyday life.  
Diversity and inclusion: At Luxid, we value different backgrounds and expertise. Our team includes professionals from various countries and cultures, and we are truly international, English-speaking, and inclusive. We believe that a diverse group brings new perspectives and strengthens our team.  
Well being and flexibility: We provide extensive occupational health care, culture, sports, and wellness benefits, as well as flexible remote work opportunities worldwide. Our offices are located in Helsinki, Turku, London, Austin and Dayton. For this position, regular visits to our Helsinki or Turku office are required.  
Competitive salary: Starting at €3800/month, based on experience and skills. 


Please apply by June 26, 2026. We review applications on a rolling basis and will fill the position as soon as we find the right candidate.
