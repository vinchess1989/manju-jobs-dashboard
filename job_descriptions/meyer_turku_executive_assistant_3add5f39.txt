Join One of the World’s Leading Shipbuilding Teams




This is your opportunity to become a member of one of the world's strongest shipbuilding teams. Join us in designing and building the world’s most advanced cruise ships and leave your mark on the history of shipbuilding!




Meyer Turku offers you an inspiring work environment where you can grow and build a career that looks like you.




We are now looking for an Executive Assistant to join our leadership team




As an Executive Assistant, you will play a vital role in ensuring the smooth daily operations of the executive and operational management teams. You will work closely with and directly support the Deputy CEO, while also providing high-quality support to selected members of the management team. In this role, you will work closely with a Senior Executive Assistant, forming a collaborative partnership to ensure seamless support for the leadership team. In close collaboration with senior stakeholders, you act as a trusted coordinator who ensures seamless planning, communication, and execution of key activities.




Your main responsibilities include:

Proactive calendar and schedule management for key stakeholders, including meeting planning and coordination
Coordination of management meetings, workshops, and events — including preparation of agendas, presentations, and supporting materials
Planning and organizing internal and external events and visitor programs, including logistics, arrangements, and hosting
Support in organizing company-level activities and executive events
Travel arrangements and related administration (bookings, expense claims, and coordination with service providers)
Invoice processing and handling of routine administrative tasks
Office administration and coordination of services and facilities (maintenance, cleaning, supplies etc.)
Close collaboration with internal and external stakeholders




What we expect from you:

In this role, you are expected to take ownership of your responsibilities and continuously develop smoother ways of working. You build strong trust with the leaders you support and bring structure, reliability, and proactivity to everything you do. With a service-minded attitude and strong attention to detail, you help create a well-organized environment where leaders can focus on strategic priorities.

You are comfortable working independently, taking initiative, and making decisions within your area of responsibility. This role suits you well if you enjoy both high-level coordination and hands-on practical work in a dynamic, fast-paced environment.




You will succeed in this role if you bring:

Solid experience in a similar role in an international, fast-paced environment (typically 5+ years)
Relevant educational background (Bachelor’s or Master’s degree)
Strong business understanding, analytical and problem-solving skills
Excellent ability to manage multiple priorities, work under pressure, and meet deadlines
Excellent communication and collaboration skills in both Finnish and English
A structured, proactive, and independent way of working with a hands-on attitude
Curiosity to learn, develop, and continuously improve ways of working
Strong Microsoft 365 skills (especially Excel, PowerPoint, and Word)
Flexibility and adaptability in a dynamic environment
A pragmatic, service-oriented mindset and willingness to handle both complex executive matters and day-to-day practical tasks




Please note that the selected candidate must pass a drug test and an occupational health examination.




Why work with us?




You will get to work at the very top of shipbuilding. The world’s most advanced cruise ships are designed and built at the Meyer Turku shipyard. We are known for our high‑tech expertise, multidisciplinary production, and innovative solutions that drive the entire shipbuilding industry forward. Our order book extends far into the future. In 2025, our revenue was EUR 2,1 billion, and we employed approximately 2,300 people.




You will be part of a team of top professionals. We have expertise that cannot be found anywhere else. Our shipyard represents over 450 different professions, and we are all shipbuilders, everyone’s contribution is essential to achieving our shared goal.




You will get a tailwind for your career. We encourage our employees to develop, learn new things, and seize opportunities. We make long careers possible: new and interesting roles are often available within our own organization.




Our employee benefits are in good order. Our own occupational health care center provides versatile services, support, and assistance throughout your career. In your free time, you can work out at Meyer Turku’s gym, take part in our hobby groups, or use our sports and cultural benefits for your other leisure activities.
