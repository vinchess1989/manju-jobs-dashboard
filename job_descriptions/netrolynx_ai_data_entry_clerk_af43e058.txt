About The Company

Great Lion is a reputable consulting firm specializing in product testing and product development services. Our mission is to connect consumers with leading brands by providing authentic, real-time feedback on new products and services. We design and conduct In-Home Usage Testing (IHUT) both locally and nationally, helping companies ensure their products meet market standards and achieve greater market access. Our commitment to privacy, security, and quality makes us a trusted partner in market research, enabling us to deliver valuable insights to our clients while offering flexible opportunities for individuals seeking supplemental income.

About The Role

We are seeking dedicated Product Testers to work remotely from anywhere across the United States. This role involves participating in various in-home product testing projects, providing honest feedback, and documenting your experiences as per project guidelines. No prior experience is required, making it an ideal opportunity for entry-level candidates, students, retirees, or anyone looking for flexible, part-time work. The position offers 15-25 hours per week, with pay rates ranging from $25 to $45 per hour, depending on the project. As a Product Tester, you will play a crucial role in helping brands refine their offerings and ensure product quality before market launch.

Qualifications

To be successful in this role, candidates should possess the following qualifications:


Ability to follow detailed instructions accurately
Strong attention to detail and a curious, engaged mindset
Reliable internet connection and access to a computer
Access to a digital camera or smartphone capable of taking clear pictures
Honesty, reliability, and good communication skills
Must be 18 years or older
Ability to commit to 15-25 hours per week and maintain a consistent routine


Responsibilities

As a Product Tester, your main duties will include:


Following the In-Home Usage Test Daily Schedule meticulously, which may involve unpacking products, reading instructions, using products responsibly, and completing journal entries
Documenting your experience through screenshots, audio recordings, videos, or written reviews, as instructed
Using products for designated periods and providing honest feedback about your experience
Participating in private chat discussions when required to share insights or clarify feedback
Taking photographs of the products and recording your usage process as needed
Adhering to all safety and usage guidelines provided by the market research firm


Benefits

This role offers numerous benefits, including:


Highly competitive hourly pay rates between $25 and $45, depending on the project
Weekly payments via direct deposit or check, providing consistent income
Flexible work schedule that adapts to your personal routine and commitments
Opportunity to learn about new products and industry trends
The convenience of working from home, eliminating commute time
Most tested products are yours to keep, offering additional value
Entry-level friendly, with no prior experience necessary
