Executive Assistant

ChaosTrack | Remote (US) | Full-Time




About ChaosTrack




ChaosTrack is an AI-powered cybersecurity preparedness and incident response platform built for modern enterprises. We help security teams run comprehensive tabletop exercises, simulate real-world breach scenarios, and automate incident response — so when chaos strikes, they're ready. We're a fast-growing startup and we're looking for an Executive Assistant to help keep our leadership team running at full speed.

The Role




This is a supporting role for our CEO and broader executive leadership team. You'll be the person who makes sure nothing falls through the cracks — owning calendars, coordinating travel, managing communications, and handling the day-to-day administrative tasks that keep things moving. If you're someone who's naturally organized, thrives in a fast-paced environment, and takes pride in being one step ahead, this role is for you.




What You'll Do




Calendar & Scheduling

Manage complex, ever-changing calendars for the CEO and members of the executive leadership team
Coordinate internal and external meetings across multiple time zones
Prioritize and protect executive time based on business needs




Travel & Logistics

Book and manage all travel arrangements — flights, hotels, ground transportation, and itineraries
Anticipate needs and handle last-minute changes with minimal disruption




Communications & Administration

Serve as a point of contact for internal and external communications on behalf of executives
Draft, proofread, and organize emails, memos, and other correspondence
Maintain organized filing systems, documents, and records
Prepare materials and agendas for meetings




General Support

Handle ad hoc requests and special projects as they come up
Support onboarding logistics and team coordination as the company grows
Identify ways to make the executive team more efficient over time




What We're Looking For

1–4 years of experience as an Executive Assistant, Administrative Assistant, or similar role
Exceptionally organized with strong attention to detail
Comfortable managing multiple priorities and shifting gears quickly
Strong written and verbal communication skills
Proactive, resourceful, and able to work independently in a remote environment
Discreet and trustworthy when handling confidential information
Experience with tools like Microsoft Office, Zoom, and HubSpot a plus
Startup experience or comfort working in a fast-moving environment is a big plus




Compensation & Benefits

Competitive salary commensurate with experience
100% remote — work from anywhere in the US
Health, dental, and vision insurance
Ongoing growth and development opportunities




How to Apply

Interested? Send your resume and a brief note about yourself to hiring@chaostrack.com. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis.
