This position is listed on behalf of a partner company, who manages all applications and next steps. Our partner is looking for an Operations Specialist (Creative Production) based in Finland.

This role sits at the heart of a fast-scaling creative production environment within a global digital health product ecosystem focused on AI-driven user experiences. You will help design and run the operational backbone that enables creative teams to deliver high-quality output at speed and scale. Acting as a bridge between production, strategy, and MarTech teams, you will bring structure, clarity, and consistency to fast-moving workflows. The role combines process design, project coordination, and hands-on operational execution. You will also play a key role in introducing automation and AI-driven solutions to reduce manual effort and improve efficiency. It is an opportunity to shape how creative production operates in a data-driven, product-led environment focused on real-world impact.

Accountabilities

In this role, you will be responsible for building and maintaining the operational systems that support creative production at scale, ensuring teams are aligned, efficient, and unblocked in their execution.


Design and maintain scalable end-to-end processes, documentation, onboarding materials, and operational frameworks that support creative production workflows.
Coordinate recurring team rhythms including weekly, monthly, and quarterly planning, ensuring clarity on priorities, ownership, and deliverables.
Improve cross-functional collaboration by aligning producers, strategists, and platform teams, and ensuring smooth information flow and dependency management.
Identify and implement AI and automation opportunities to reduce repetitive operational tasks such as reporting, tracking, scheduling, and documentation.
Partner with MarTech and AI teams to build smarter, more efficient operational systems and workflow enhancements.


Requirements

You bring strong operational discipline and a systems-thinking mindset, with the ability to structure complexity and improve how work gets done across teams.


Experience in operations, project management, production operations, or business operations in a fast-paced environment.
Proven ability to build processes, systems, and documentation from scratch and continuously improve them.
Strong organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple workflows and stakeholders simultaneously.
Excellent communication and coordination skills across cross-functional teams and different levels of seniority.
Strong attention to detail with a high degree of accountability and follow-through.
Systems-thinking mindset with the ability to identify bottlenecks, inefficiencies, and automation opportunities.
Strong interest in AI, automation tools, and modern operational platforms (Airtable, Notion, project management tools, or similar).
English proficiency at B2 level or above.


Benefits


Competitive salary package aligned with experience and market standards
Flexible work arrangements (remote, hybrid, or in-office depending on preference and setup)
Provided work equipment to support productivity
Access to a premium subscription to the product ecosystem
21 days of annual leave plus local public holidays
Flexible working hours focused on outcomes rather than time spent working
Exposure to AI-driven operational tooling and modern creative production systems
Opportunity to work in a high-growth, mission-driven digital health environment
