LHH Recruitment Solutions is seeking a Compliance Consultant at our top tier financial services client in Philadelphia, PA.




Description

• Maintenance of the employee compliance reporting system (including but not limited to testing and inputting transaction details)

• New employee hire and termination processes

• Conduct ad hoc due diligence requests on counterparties, vendors, and other third parties

• Maintenance of the firm's restricted list (including updating for new restrictions)

• Monitoring employee trade activity

• Data entry, exception review and issue tracking

• Review and analysis of employee certifications

• Testing required by regulators

• Monthly cybersecurity review of vendors

• Code of Business Conduct and Ethics monitoring and testing

• Assist with regulatory filings and documentation requests

• Form ADV reviews of sub-advisers

• Monthly and quarterly testing memos on a variety of topics

• Administration of the firm's AI Governance Committee

• Disclosure library for international jurisdictions

• Special and ad hoc projects as they frequently arise
