Räätälöi ansioluetteloni

Our client, a large player in Medical Equipment Manufacturing and Medtech is looking for an Internal Communications contractor to join their team on a 6-month contract basis, starting immediately.




Location: 100% remote

Hours: 24 hours/week

Pay Range: $40/hr




Position Summary

We are seeking a highly organized and proactive Internal Communications Contractor to support the planning, development, and execution of employee communications and engagement initiatives. This role will help create compelling content, support communication campaigns, and coordinate a recurring company-wide knowledge sharing program designed to connect employees with business insights, innovations, projects, and best practices across the organizatio

n.The ideal candidate is a strong writer, skilled project manager, and collaborative communicator who can work effectively with leaders, subject matter experts, and cross-functional teams to deliver engaging and informative employee experiences.




Key Responsibilities

Internal Communications Content Development

Develop and edit employee-facing communications, including articles, announcements, newsletters, intranet content, leader messages, and presentations
Create communications that translate complex topics into clear, engaging, and actionable content
Maintain communication calendars and support planning for key organizational announcements and campaigns
Ensure consistency of messaging, tone, and brand standards across communication channels
Partner with stakeholders to gather information, interview subject matter experts, and draft content that highlights business priorities and employee stories




Knowledge Sharing Program Management (Primary Deliverable)

Own the programming, planning, and production of a recurring employee knowledge sharing session designed for all employees




Responsibilities include:

Develop and manage an annual editorial and programming calendar
Identify and recruit presenters and subject matter experts from across the organization
Collaborate with speakers to shape content, key messages, and presentation flow
Coordinate session logistics including scheduling, technology setup, promotional communications, registration, and audience engagement
Create promotional materials and employee awareness campaigns to drive attendance
Moderate sessions as needed and facilitate audience Q&A
Manage recording, post-event distribution, and content archiving
Track participation and feedback metrics and provide recommendations for continuous improvement
Develop repeatable processes and templates that support long-term program sustainability




Employee Engagement Support

Support employee engagement initiatives and communication campaigns
Assist with content curation and storytelling to showcase organizational achievements, innovation, and collaboration
Help identify opportunities to elevate employee voices and knowledge sharing across the business




Qualifications Required

Bachelor's degree in Communications, Public Relations, Journalism, Marketing, or related field, or equivalent experience
5-8+ years of experience in internal communications, corporate communications, content development, or employee engagement
Exceptional writing, editing, and proofreading skills
Strong project management and organizational skills with the ability to manage multiple priorities simultaneously
Experience coordinating virtual or hybrid events, webinars, or employee programs
Ability to work independently while collaborating effectively with stakeholders at all levels




Preferred Experience

Supporting communications in a corporate or manufacturing environment
Familiarity with Microsoft 365 tools, including Teams, SharePoint, Viva Engage, PowerPoint, and Outlook
Experience developing communications plans and measuring engagement metrics
Basic graphic design or presentation development skills
