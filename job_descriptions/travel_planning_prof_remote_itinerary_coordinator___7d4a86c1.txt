Position Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented Itinerary Coordinator to assist with organizing and managing travel plans and schedules. This role focuses on structuring itineraries, coordinating details, and ensuring all arrangements are accurate and aligned with client needs.

This is a fully remote position ideal for individuals who enjoy planning, organizing timelines, and working in a client-focused environment.

Key Responsibilities


Create and organize detailed itineraries based on client preferences and schedules
Coordinate travel details including timing, reservations, and activities
Review itinerary components to ensure accuracy and completeness
Communicate updates, confirmations, and important details clearly
Maintain organized records of itineraries and client information
Monitor timelines and ensure all arrangements are properly aligned




Qualifications


Strong organizational and time management skills
Excellent communication and attention to detail
Ability to work independently in a remote setting
Comfortable managing multiple itineraries and deadlines
Basic computer skills and ability to navigate online systems




Preferred (Not Required)


Experience in coordination, scheduling, or customer service roles
Interest in travel, planning, or hospitality-related services




What We Offer


Flexible remote work environment
Structured onboarding and ongoing support
Opportunities for growth based on performance
Supportive and collaborative team environment




Work Environment
This is a remote role requiring a reliable internet connection and the ability to stay organized and self-directed in a virtual workspace.
