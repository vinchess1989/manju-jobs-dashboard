Flexjet and its portfolio of sister brands are seeking an experienced, highly organized, and hospitality-minded Event Coordinator to help bring our brand to life through thoughtfully curated, high-touch experiences and engagements. This role is instrumental in executing events that deepen Owner relationships, strengthen brand affinity, create meaningful prospect connections and reinforce Flexjet's position as one of the world's most distinctive luxury brands.

Serving as a steward of hospitality and brand experience, the Event Coordinator will bring precision, creativity and exceptional attention to detail to every interaction. From intimate Owner gatherings and aircraft showcases to philanthropic galas, strategic partnerships and internal celebrations, this role ensures every experience reflects the level of care, sophistication and service our Owners and guests have come to expect.

Working collaboratively across Marketing, Sales, Global Expansion and EMEA teams—as well as with external agencies, venues and vendors—the Event Coordinator will translate strategic objectives into memorable experiences that foster lasting relationships and deliver measurable business impact.

coordination, invitations, registration processes, travel arrangements, run-of-show documentation, guest communications and on-site execution


Ensure all event elements reflect Flexjet’s luxury standards and deliver a cohesive, elevated brand experience
Maintain detailed project timelines and ensure all deliverables are completed accurately and on schedule


Owner Experience & Hospitality

Project & Budget Management

Prepare event documentation, including run-of-

Compile event metrics and reporting data to support quarterly and annual performance reviews


Vendor & Partner Coordination


Coordinate and direct vendors and external partners during event planning and on-site execution
Maintain day-to-day relationships with venues, agencies, promotional partners and hospitality vendors
Conduct site visits and participate in vendor meetings to ensure operational readiness and executional excellence


Operational Support & Continuous Enhancement


Identify opportunities to improve event processes, guest engagement and operational efficiency
Coordinate ordering, organization, inventory and distribution of branded merchandise and promotional materials for Owners, guests and employees
Support additional marketing initiatives and special projects as business needs evolve within a fast-paced, collaborative environment
