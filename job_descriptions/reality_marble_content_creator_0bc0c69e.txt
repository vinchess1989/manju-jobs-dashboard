Content Creator

Mini Katana is looking for full-time content creators based in the United States to produce short-form videos for our social media channels.




This is not an influencer sponsorship or brand deal. You will be creating content directly for our channels as part of a growing creator team.




YouTube Channel Link: https://www.youtube.com/@MiniKatanaStore




What You'll Do
Create engaging short-form videos for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, Instagram Reels, and Facebook
Film yourself on camera demonstrating products, challenges, skits, trends, and creative concepts
Produce content consistently on a weekly basis
Collaborate with our team to test new ideas and improve performance
Help grow one of the largest creator-led brands in the collectibles and anime space




We're Looking For People Who
Are comfortable on camera
Understand what makes short-form content perform well
Have experience creating content for TikTok, YouTube Shorts, or Instagram Reels
Can consistently produce high-quality videos
Are interested in pursuing content creation as a full-time career
A large following is not required. We care more about your ability to create engaging content than your audience size.




Nice to Have
Film, media, or content creation background
Experience editing short-form videos
Interest in anime, gaming, collectibles, or pop culture
Experience working with brands or creating UGC content




Why Join Us
Create content as your full-time job
Work directly with an established brand and large social media audience
Freedom to experiment with content ideas and formats




To Apply

Please send:

Examples of your best content
Links to your social media accounts
A brief introduction about yourself and your content creation experience
We encourage applicants from all backgrounds and experience levels. If you know how to make engaging content and are excited about creating videos full-time, we'd love to hear from you.
