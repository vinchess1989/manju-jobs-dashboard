Personal Assistant to Entrepreneur




A forward thinking and fast-moving entrepreneur is looking for a Personal Assistant to help keep his schedule and life on track. This role will be responsible for coordinating all personal appointments and scheduling, booking international and domestic travel itineraries, and keeping him abreast of deadlines, running errands, assisting with household organization and projects and more. He is seeking someone who is resourceful, quick on their feet and New York savvy.




This role will be primarily remote but need the ability to meet up once a week or so in the Soho area.




Tasks include, but are not limited to:

Managing busy personal and social calendars
Running general errands, gift purchasing, shopping and returns, grocery shopping
Making appointments
Setting up complex travel arrangements, making reservations, creating itineraries, researching prices and options
Planning events and entertainment
Managing vendors for the home
Maintaining correspondence
Assisting with personal projects
Coordinating with children at college for needs




Other desired skillset and qualifications:

NYC based - familiarity with NYC vendors
High energy and Intuitive
Strong interpersonal skills
Tech-savvy/experience with G-suite and email
Proactive approach to problem-solving
Enjoys Personal Assistant duties
Ability to multitask
Strong time-management and organizational skills
Detail-oriented




Compensation based upon experience
