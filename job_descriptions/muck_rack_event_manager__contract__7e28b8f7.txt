Muck Rack is the leading SaaS platform for public relations and communications professionals. Our mission is to enable organizations to build trust, tell their stories, and demonstrate the unique value of earned media. Muck Rack’s AI-powered, comprehensive, and integrated platform streamlines the PR workflow to help businesses generate positive media coverage, monitor mentions to manage brand reputation, and analyze PR’s impact on business outcomes. By combining media database, monitoring, and reporting into one dynamic platform, we empower teams to collaborate seamlessly, pitch effectively, and analyze results faster and more efficiently.

Founder-controlled, fully distributed, and growing sustainably, Muck Rack has received several awards for its unparalleled culture and product from organizations like Inc., Quartz, G2, and BuiltIn. We value resilience, transparency, ownership, and customer devotion, and infuse these values into everything we do. 

Muck Rack is seeking an experienced Event Manager to support the planning and execution of the Generative Pulse Summit 2026. This role will provide critical administrative and production support to ensure a seamless and successful Summit including event production, speaker manager, and onsite showrunning and room management. This contractor will work closely with the Sr. Manager of Events & Partnerships and cross-functional teams to manage logistics, communications, and on-site execution.

What you’ll do:
Project Management
Manage and update tasks in project management platform, ensuring timelines and deliverables stay on track
Maintain accurate documentation across all event workstreams
Registration & Data Management
Support registration tracking and reporting
Speaker Coordination
Provide administrative support for speaker communications
Assist with outreach, scheduling, and collection of speaker releases
Run of Show (RoS) Management
Maintain and update the master run of show with all relevant event details
Ensure alignment across stakeholders on timing and programming
Budget Tracking
Keep the event budget document up to date
Track expenses and flag variances as needed
Website Coordination
Compile and communicate daily website updates to Engineering
Ensure event information is accurate and current
Vendor & Venue Coordination
Liaise with venue and external vendors to support logistics and execution
Assist with contract deliverables and day-to-day coordination
Event Production
Support logistics planning
Support design and decor production
Support A/V production
Support registration setup and administration
On-Site Execution
Oversee room management and session logistics
Manage the main session room run of show and coordinate A/V execution
If the details below describe you, you could be a great fit for this role:
3+ years of event coordination or event management experience in conferences or corporate events
Strong organizational and project management skills with attention to detail
Experience with tools such as Asana, Salesforce, and event registration platforms
Excellent written and verbal communication skills
Ability to manage multiple priorities in a fast-paced environment
Experience supporting live, in-person event execution, including A/V coordination

In the US, the hourly rate for this role is $32/hour, with an average of 20 hours per week, plus 12 hours onsite at the event.
