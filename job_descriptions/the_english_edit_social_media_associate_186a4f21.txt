About us: 

The English Edit is a creative e-commerce agency built on years of in-house experience at high-fashion and retail brands in New York City. We partner with brands of all sizes, ranging from emerging labels to global names like HOKA, UGG, and Hunza G, to deliver results-driven, creatively led e-commerce experiences. Our ethos is simple: transforming brands with creative e-commerce, powered by strong relationships and a highly collaborative approach. 




About the role:

We are looking for a Social Media Associate to support content creation, publishing, and community engagement across multiple client accounts. This is a hands-on role for someone who lives and breathes social media, understands fashion and retail culture, and is excited to help brands show up consistently and creatively across platforms.




You will work closely with senior strategists, designers, and marketing leads to bring social narratives to life for the in-house agency account, as well as various client accounts.




Responsibilities: 

Support day-to-day social media management across Instagram, TikTok, and other key platforms
Assist in content planning, scheduling, and publishing for multiple client brands
Create on-brand social assets in collaboration with design and creative teams
Write engaging captions aligned with each brand’s voice and messaging
Monitor comments, DMs, and community interactions to foster positive engagement
Track performance metrics and assist with reporting and insights
Stay current on platform trends, cultural moments, and emerging formats




What we’re looking for: 

2 to 3 years of experience in social media, content creation, or digital marketing
Experience working with fashion, retail, or lifestyle brands preferred
Proficiency in Instagram, TikTok, Canva, Figma, CapCut, or Adobe Creative Suite
Strong copywriting skills and a sharp eye for visual storytelling and graphic design
Ability to manage multiple accounts while staying organized and detail-oriented
Excellent communication skills and a collaborative mindset
Comfortable being on camera and client-facing 
Bachelor’s degree in Marketing, Communications, Media, or a related field preferred
