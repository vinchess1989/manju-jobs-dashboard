Official Job description




This role falls under the Quality, Research, & Measurement Department and will report to the Performance Measurement Principal. Tasks include verifying submitted data, preparing data for aggregation, and finalizing reports on the aggregated data.




Tasks:

Compare data on Excel file to data submitted on a PDF and note any differences in the Excel file (Oct)
Use a VLOOKUP in Excel to compare list of ~700 respondents required to report data to the list of respondents received and note variances (Sept/Oct)
Send Login/Passwords to accredited organizations who need to submit data, as needed via email (Sept/Oct)
Aggregate Reports (Nov/Dec)
Map data file to be able to complete a mail merge in MS Word and then convert to PDF
Ensure formatting is correct and make any edits in PDF
Split PDF into 700+ individual reports
Rename 700+ files according to provided syntax




Skills:

Excel (VLOOKUP, Sorting, Check for Duplicate, able to navigate Pivot Table)
PDF/ MS Word (mail merge from a data file, combining documents, editing and formatting, and splitting large documents into smaller client specific documents)
Detail Oriented to ensure confidentiality of client data is not sent to other organizations
Communication (frequent updates on progress)
Strong organizational and time management skills with demonstrated flexibility and the ability to manage multiple tasks and issues of varying complexity, while managing priorities and meeting deadlines.
Strong written and verbal communication skills with the ability to generate original correspondence and reports and convey ideas clearly and effectively.
