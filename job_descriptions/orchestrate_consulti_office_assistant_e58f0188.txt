What To Expect (Job Responsibilities)


Answer phones and emails, providing prompt and professional communication
Complete entry-level bookkeeping tasks, including recording expenses and organizing receipts
Resolve billing issues with clients and internal team members
Support quality assurance checks of various internal and client-facing reports
Organize new client contracts, create invoices, and process client payments


What Is Required (Qualifications)


Willingness to learn, grow, and collaborate with the team
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
A high level of discretion, ethics, and trustworthiness
Availability to work 40 hours per week from 9:00 am to 6:00 pm PST
A reliable remote work setup with a fast computer, quality internet, camera, microphone, and speakers


How To Stand Out (Preferred Qualifications)


Experience in bookkeeping or financial record keeping
Intermediate spreadsheet skills
Familiarity with Google Sheets or Excel, Quickbooks Online, and G-Suite
Innovative thinking and a willingness to challenge existing methods for improvement
