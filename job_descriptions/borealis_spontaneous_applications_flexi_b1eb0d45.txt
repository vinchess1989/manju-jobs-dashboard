Spontaneous Application

At Borealis, we are always looking for talented and qualified people to join our company. We are proud of the work that we do, and wouldn't be successful without our great team of dedicated people.

If you are flexible about your place of work, please submit your resume for "Spontaneous Application Flexible Location". You can, of course, indicate in your candidate profile to what extent you are flexible and which locations/countries/regions you would prefer. If you want to spontaneously apply for a specific country/region, please go back to the overview and choose the country/region you are interested in.

We will review your information and contact you if any relevant positions become available. We kindly invite you to also monitor our vacancies - e.g. via a job alert that is available in your candidate profile - and actively apply for vacancies that awake your interest.
