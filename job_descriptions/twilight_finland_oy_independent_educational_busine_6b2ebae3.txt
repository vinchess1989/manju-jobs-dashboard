Työn kuvaus

Join Twilight Finland's Mission to Transform Education Globally About Twilight Finland Twilight Finland Oy is a Finland-based international education organization committed to building meaningful global connections through education, innovation, professional development, academic excellence, and international collaboration. As Twilight Finland continues to expand its global educational footprint, we are inviting passionate and motivated individuals to join our network of Independent Educational Business Associates and contribute to promoting world-class educational opportunities within their communities.

About the Opportunity

This opportunity is ideal for individuals who are passionate about education and are committed to expanding access to innovative learning experiences for students, educators, and educational institutions in emerging and international markets beyond Europe. As an Independent Educational Business Associate, you will act as a strategic link between educational institutions across international markets outside Europe and Twilight Finland's globally focused educational programs, initiatives, and opportunities. You will introduce the programs designed by Twilight Finland to our potential customer base which are schools, educators, students, parents, and educational organizations. This role offers flexibility, independence, professional growth, and the opportunity to create a positive impact in the education sector while building an additional income stream.

Programs You May Promote

Depending on the agreed objectives and market requirements, you will be involved in promoting the following educational programs and services: Student Educational Tours School Partnership Programs

Key Responsibilities

As an Educational Business Associate, you will: - Build relationships with schools, colleges, educational institutions, and educators. - Introduce Twilight Finland's educational programs to potential participants. - Generate leads and referrals through professional networks. - Organize introductory meetings and presentations. - Support outreach initiatives within your region. - Coordinate with the Twilight Finland team regarding interested institutions. - Identify new opportunities for educational collaboration. - Promote educational events, webinars, and information sessions. - Represent Twilight Finland professionally within your local educational community.

What We Offer

- Attractive commission-based earnings. - Flexible work arrangements. - Professional onboarding and training. - Marketing and promotional support. - Access to internationally recognized educational programs. - Opportunity to work with a Finland-based education organization. - Professional networking opportunities. - Recognition and performance-based rewards. - Potential long-term collaboration opportunities for high-performing associates.

Ideal Candidate Profile

We are looking for individuals who: - Are passionate about education. - Have strong communication and networking skills. - Enjoy building professional relationships. - Are self-motivated and goal-oriented. - Can represent educational programs professionally. - Have basic digital communication skills. - Believe in creating opportunities for students and educators.

Compensation

This is a commission-based independent opportunity. Associates earn commissions based on successful program registrations, institutional partnerships, and completed enrollments according to the applicable commission structure. There is no fixed salary associated with this opportunity. Outstanding performers may be considered for future leadership roles, regional partnerships, or long-term professional engagement with Twilight Finland. Important Notice This opportunity is offered on an independent associate basis and does not constitute employment. Selected applicants may be required to complete onboarding formalities and sign an Independent Business Associate Agreement prior to commencing activities.

Lisätietoja yrityksestä

Twilight Finland is expanding internationally and is seeking ambitious, well-connected individuals who want to build a rewarding income stream through education and international opportunities.

This is not a traditional job with a fixed salary. Instead, it is a performance-based opportunity where your earnings are directly linked to your results. The more relationships you build and the more opportunities you create, the greater your earning potential.

If you have connections with schools, educators, students, parents, educational institutions, community organizations, or professional networks, this opportunity can provide substantial commission earnings while allowing you to work independently and flexibly.
