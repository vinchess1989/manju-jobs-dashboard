Winthrop Technologies is Europe's only dedicated data centre delivery partner, offering full turnkey solutions from design through to construction and commissioning. Headquartered in Dublin, we deliver projects across Europe and have become the most trusted name in our sector. 

  

We have an opportunity for a Document Controller on a major data centre project in Finland.  This role is vital to supporting the smooth operation of a fast-paced construction site environment, ensuring the effective management and control of all project documentation in accordance with company standards. 



Requirements


Ensure all project documentation adheres to company standards and the Integrated Management System (IMS).  
Distribute and track documents to ensure that all relevant stakeholders receive and have access to the latest versions of project documentation (including drawings, schedules, specifications, contracts, submittals, RFIs, and reports).  
Maintain and update project registers such as the document register, RFI register, action log, and issue log.  
Analyse and summarise data from various project logs, highlighting outstanding actions or overdue items.  
Generate regular reports to support project tracking, including document status updates and overdue reports.  
Ensure accurate storage and version control of documents in compliance with project document control procedures.  
Liaise with external consultants and contractors to ensure their documentation complies with project standards.  
Support Quality Assurance (QA), Quality Control (QC), and Health & Safety (HSE) initiatives by assisting with audits and ensuring document-related compliance. 


Skills/Qualifications:

Minimum of 2 years' experience in a similar site-based role 
High level of verbal and written English is essential  
Excellent organisation and interpersonal skills 
High level of IT skills, particularly in Microsoft Office Suite 
Experience of knowledge sharing applications is advantageous  



Benefits


Opportunity to work for a market leader in the Data centre industry  
Working on large scale data centre projects 
Collaborative team working environments 
Career progression and development supported 
Competitive salary packages
