Position Overview
We are seeking a detail-oriented and organized Event & Travel Coordinator to assist with coordinating event arrangements and travel-related services. This role focuses on managing logistics, organizing schedules, and ensuring all details are handled efficiently to support a seamless client experience.

This is a work-from-home position ideal for individuals who enjoy planning, coordination, and working in a structured, client-focused environment.

Key Responsibilities


Coordinate event details alongside travel-related arrangements and scheduling
Assist with organizing timelines, reservations, and logistics
Communicate with clients to gather information and confirm plans
Review and verify details to ensure accuracy and completeness
Provide updates, confirmations, and follow-ups in a timely manner
Maintain organized records of client plans and coordination activities




Qualifications:



Strong organizational and multitasking skills
Excellent communication and customer service abilities
Ability to work independently and manage time effectively
Detail-oriented with strong follow-through
Comfortable using digital tools, email, and scheduling platforms




Preferred (Not Required)


Experience in event coordination, travel planning, or customer service
Interest in events, hospitality, or travel-related services




What We Offer:



Flexible work-from-home environment
Structured onboarding and ongoing support
Opportunities for growth and advancement based on performance
Supportive and collaborative team environment




Work Environment
This is a remote role requiring a reliable internet connection and the ability to stay organized and productive while working independently.
