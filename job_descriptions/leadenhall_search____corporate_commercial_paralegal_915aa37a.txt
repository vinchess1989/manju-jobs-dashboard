A mission-driven technology company operating in a highly regulated environment is seeking a Paralegal to join its growing legal team. This is an opportunity to work closely with experienced lawyers on a wide range of commercial, compliance, and operational legal matters while supporting a business tackling complex, real-world challenges.




What You'll Do

Draft, review, and manage a high volume of legal documents, including NDAs and other routine commercial agreements.
Support commercial contracting processes across the business.
Assist with large-scale legal, compliance, and operational projects.
Maintain contract records, legal documentation, and tracking systems.
Coordinate with internal stakeholders to gather information, manage workflows, and ensure timely execution of legal processes.
Support multiple lawyers across a variety of corporate and commercial matters.
Help improve legal operations, processes, and documentation practices as the company continues to scale.




What They're Looking For

Early-Career Candidates
Bachelor's degree required.
1–2 years of paralegal or relevant legal experience.
Strong academic background and demonstrated intellectual curiosity.
Interest in building a long-term career within an in-house legal team.




Experienced Career Paralegals
Several years of experience supporting corporate, commercial, or compliance-focused legal teams.
Ability to manage matters independently and take ownership of projects.
Comfortable working directly with attorneys and business stakeholders across multiple functions.




Preferred Background

Experience supporting corporate and commercial legal work.
Experience in highly regulated industries such as:
Technology
Healthcare
Life Sciences
Financial Services
Energy or Natural Resources
Familiarity with contracts, compliance processes, and legal operations.
Experience handling NDAs, commercial agreements, and contract management systems.




Key Qualities

They're looking for someone who is:

Highly organized and detail-oriented.
Bright, proactive, and eager to learn.
Able to manage multiple priorities and meet deadlines consistently.
An excellent communicator who keeps stakeholders informed and aligned.
Comfortable working independently in a remote environment.
Resourceful, dependable, and capable of operating with minimal supervision.
This is not a litigation role, so litigation experience is not needed here




Why Join?

Broad exposure to commercial, compliance, and operational legal matters.
Opportunity to work closely with experienced attorneys and business leaders.
Significant responsibility and ownership from day one.
A collaborative, mission-driven culture with opportunities for long-term growth and development.
The chance to help build and improve legal processes within a fast-growing organization.




This role is ideal for a motivated paralegal who enjoys problem-solving, thrives in a fast-paced environment, and wants to make a meaningful impact while continuing to develop their career.
