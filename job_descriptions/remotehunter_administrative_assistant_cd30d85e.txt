1. About Our Client:

AOM Infusion is a provider in the home and specialty infusion therapy industry. The organization focuses on delivering patient-centered care through experienced clinicians and professionals, aiming to ensure safe, compassionate, and personalized treatment across the communities it serves.




2. About the Opportunity:

The Administrative Assistant supports the Nursing Department by facilitating efficient departmental operations through clerical and administrative tasks. This role contributes to smooth administrative processes, enabling the department to function effectively under supervision.




3. Responsibilities:

• Perform clerical duties and administrative processes as assigned.

• Retrieve and summarize information from records, emails, and related documents.

• Carry out filing, typing, copying, binding, scanning, and reporting tasks.

• Assist with maintaining the document database.

• Respond to and resolve administrative inquiries.

• Prepare photocopies and print documents for colleagues.

• Provide polite and professional communication.

• Conduct data entry.

• Cover the reception desk as needed.

• Manage multiple projects concurrently.

• Perform other related duties as assigned.




4. Requirements:

• High school diploma or equivalent.

• At least one year of experience as an Administrative Assistant, preferably in healthcare.

• Proficiency in Microsoft Word and Excel.

• Strong communication, telephone, and organizational skills.




5. Pay Range and Compensation Package:

• The pay range and compensation package for this role will be determined based on the candidate’s experience, skills, and other relevant factors.




6. Benefits & Perks:

• Medical insurance

• Vision insurance

• 401(k) plan

• Paid time off and paid holidays

• Flexible spending account (FSA)

• Life and AD&D insurance

• Disability coverage

• Employee referral program
