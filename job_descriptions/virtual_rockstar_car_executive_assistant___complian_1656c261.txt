Job Description


Virtual Rockstar is hiring a full-time Executive Assistant & Compliance Coordinator - Virtual Assistant on behalf of a growing pediatric therapy practice.


This role is a blend of executive/administrative support and compliance coordination — managing recurring administrative, HR, and compliance tasks so that leadership can focus on high-level strategy and growth. The ideal candidate is extremely detail-oriented, tech-savvy, and a strong written communicator who can be trusted with sensitive HR, financial, and HIPAA-adjacent information.


This is an excellent opportunity for a proactive self-starter who wants to be a true, reliable extension of a small but high-performing leadership team at a mission-driven pediatric healthcare practice.


About Our Client


Our client is Skills on the Hill (SOTH), a pediatric occupational therapy, physical therapy, and speech-language pathology practice with clinic locations in Washington D.C. and Arlington, Virginia, plus school-based contracts across the DMV area. Founded in 2003, their mission is to empower children and families to climb to their fullest potential.

Key Responsibilities


Administrative & Executive Support


Track action items and follow-ups from leadership and staff meetings


Prepare meeting agendas and distribute notes


Maintain document organization and version control on SharePoint


Research topics and compile summary reports as assigned


Draft routine communications from templates


Manage email inbox triage and calendar scheduling for leadership (Phase 2)


Maintain and reformat the SOP and training library on SharePoint


HR & Payroll Administration


Verify productive hours against payroll tracking and flag discrepancies


Launch annual review forms in Paylocity on schedule


Prepare HR documents from templates and maintain organized digital filing


Set up new employees in Practice Pro per onboarding checklist


Monitor Paylocity workflows and run audits to ensure records stay accurate


Compile staff survey results for leadership review


Compliance & Credentialing Support


Run OIG exclusion checks on schedule and file documentation


Maintain a compliance calendar, sending reminders and tracking completion


Conduct quarterly IT system checks using established workflows


Manage CAQH profile maintenance and attestations for clinicians


Monitor licensure and credentialing renewal dates with a tracker and reminders


Run weekly missing documentation and charges reports and notify staff


Operations, Marketing & Patient Experience


Pull and format weekly reports in Excel for leadership review


Coordinate vendor calls and track equipment/facilities issues


Create Canva content for marketing and internal communications


Draft staff newsletters and submit website updates to the marketing team


Maintain patient nurture email sequences and compile satisfaction survey results


Track referral source data and developmental screening submissions


Tools & Systems


Practice Pro (EMR)


Paylocity (HR/Payroll)


Microsoft 365 (Outlook, SharePoint, Teams, OneDrive)


CAQH


Canva


Zoom & Fathom


JAMF Pro / Apple Business Manager (training provided)


Practice Promotions / "Basecamp" (training provided)





Requirements



Strong executive or administrative assistant background, ideally in healthcare or a similarly regulated industry


Extremely detail-oriented — comfortable double-checking work where errors carry real consequences


Excellent written English communication skills — will draft communications on the practice's behalf


Proactive and reliable — takes ownership without needing constant direction


Tech-savvy and comfortable learning new platforms quickly


Experience with Practice Pro, Paylocity, Canva, or GoHighLevel is a strong advantage


Comfortable handling HIPAA-adjacent and sensitive HR/financial information with discretion


Available and responsive during Eastern Time business hours via Zoom and email


Non-Negotiables


Extreme attention to detail — errors in payroll, credentialing, or compliance have real consequences


Strong written English communication — must represent the organization professionally


Proactive, reliable, and dedicated — a true self-starter who follows through without prompting





Benefits



Competitive salary commensurate with experience.


Opportunities for professional development and growth.


Work in a dynamic and supportive team environment.


Make a meaningful impact by helping to build and strengthen families across the Globe
