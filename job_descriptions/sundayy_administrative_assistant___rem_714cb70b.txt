About The Company

Halco Lighting Technologies is a leading provider of innovative lighting solutions, committed to delivering high-quality products and exceptional customer service. With a strong reputation in the lighting industry, we specialize in manufacturing and distributing a wide range of lighting products for commercial, residential, and industrial applications. Our company values innovation, integrity, and teamwork, fostering a dynamic work environment that encourages professional growth and development. As we continue to expand our operations, we are dedicated to maintaining a collaborative culture that prioritizes customer satisfaction and sustainable business practices.

About The Role

We are seeking a reliable and detail-oriented Administrative Assistant to join our team in a fully remote capacity. This role is vital in supporting our management and department personnel by providing comprehensive administrative assistance. The ideal candidate will be responsible for coordinating daily business activities, maintaining accurate records, and assisting with various operational functions. Working independently in a professional remote environment, the Administrative Assistant will ensure smooth communication and efficient workflow across different teams. This position offers an excellent opportunity for individuals with strong organizational skills, excellent communication abilities, and the capacity to manage multiple priorities effectively. The successful candidate will play a key role in supporting our company's administrative needs while contributing to a productive and positive remote work culture.

Qualifications


High School Diploma or GED required
Associate's degree in Business Administration or a related field preferred
Minimum 1 year of administrative, office support, customer service, or related experience preferred
Strong proficiency in Microsoft Office Suite (Word, Excel, Outlook, PowerPoint)
Excellent verbal and written communication skills
Strong organizational and multitasking abilities
High attention to detail and accuracy
Ability to work independently with minimal supervision
Reliable internet connection and dedicated remote workspace required


Responsibilities


Provide administrative support to management and department personnel
Manage calendars, appointments, schedules, and meeting coordination
Respond to emails and correspondence in a timely and professional manner
Prepare reports, presentations, spreadsheets, and business documents
Maintain electronic files, records, and databases
Perform data entry and ensure accuracy of company information
Assist with document preparation, recordkeeping, and filing activities
Coordinate internal communications and administrative processes
Support recruiting, onboarding, and employee documentation activities as needed
Track projects, deadlines, and follow-up actions
Assist with invoice processing, expense tracking, and general office administration
Handle confidential information with discretion and professionalism
Perform other administrative duties and special projects as assigned
