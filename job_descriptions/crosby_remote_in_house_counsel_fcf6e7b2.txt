Crosby A.I., backed by top investors like Sequoia Capital and Bain Capital Ventures, is a successful, groundbreaking legaltech startup building both an agentic A.I. tool to assist with contract automation, as well as the law firm that uses those tools. Instead of selling our proprietary technology to other firms we are competing with them by providing fast, efficient contract negotiation to high growth companies with lean legal teams. The entire process at Crosby is overseen by our elite in-house lawyers ensuring quality and efficiency.




After a significant funding round we're seeking a driven Commercial Counsel with SaaS and tech transactions experience to be an early hire for our legal team. You'll be helping to shape our A.I. tools and using them - running commercial deals for some of the fastest growing, most innovative startups in the world.




Must be admitted to the bar in the U.S.




Transactional/contract drafting experience required. Drafting/Negotiating/Redlining sales agreements, dealing with data usage/ownership/privacy issues, familiarity with MSA's/ NDA's/DPA's/Order Forms, negotiating on 1st and 3rd party paper.




You'll join a great culture while maintaining quality work, with no billable hours. This role can be entirely remote anywhere in Singapore.




You'll have the opportunity to:




Be at the forefront of A.I. and legal tech using our latest tools and helping to shape those tools.




Gain significant experience negotiating cutting edge commercial agreements for clients that are regularly in the headlines. Our clients are some of the most innovative companies in the world.




Work entirely remote, with a set schedule, without having to bill your time.




Qualifications




Must be admitted to the bar in the U.S.




Transactional/contract drafting experience.




J.D. or LLM is ok.




For an info call please apply to this ad or email Founding Legal Recruiter Henry Klein at henry@crosbylegal.com.
