Events Coordinator

At Summit, we build and operate the infrastructure behind some of the most critical applications in regulated industries. The work is complex, expectations are high, and the environments we support require precision and thoughtful execution.




We specialize in enterprise-class Application Hosting, Managed Services, and Cloud Solutions, with deep expertise in compliance, security, and performance. Our focus is simple: make the complex manageable and deliver technology that works - reliably, securely, and at scale.




At Summit, we’re looking for people who can bring order to moving pieces, keep things on track, and help create experiences that feel seamless from start to finish. As an Events Coordinator, you’ll support the planning and execution of both virtual and in-person events that strengthen relationships, support business initiatives, and elevate the Summit brand experience.

In this role, you’ll help coordinate the operational details behind conferences, trade shows, customer events, webinars, internal meetings, and partner engagements across multiple business units and audiences. From vendor coordination and shipping logistics to event communications and onsite support, you’ll play a key role in helping events run smoothly while balancing timelines, budgets, priorities, and stakeholder needs across a busy event calendar.

The ideal candidate is highly organized, detail-oriented, adaptable, and comfortable managing multiple priorities in fast-paced environments. Success in this role comes from staying proactive, communicating clearly, and bringing a positive, team-first attitude even when timelines shift or event details change.

What You'll Do:

Coordinate logistics for conferences, trade shows, customer events, webinars, internal meetings, and partner engagements
Support the execution of 30+ events annually across multiple business units, audiences, and event types
Manage vendor relationships for venues, catering, A/V, transportation, shipping, swag, and event-related services
Support attendee registrations, communications, lead collection tools, and onsite check-in processes
Coordinate shipping, tracking, and delivery of event materials, technology equipment, and branded collateral
Build and maintain event timelines, budgets, project trackers, and task lists to keep initiatives moving forward
Partner with teams across Marketing, Sales, Product, Partner, and Operations teams to align event goals, messaging, and execution with company priorities and KPIs
Help manage and maintain an overarching enterprise event calendar to support visibility, planning, and coordination across the organization
Provide onsite and virtual event support, helping troubleshoot issues and keep events running smoothly throughout execution
Maintain inventory of event materials, giveaways, branded collateral, and event technology equipment
Manage the company swag store, including inventory coordination and fulfillment support
Identify opportunities to improve organization, processes, and the overall event experience

What You'll Deliver:

Well-executed events that create a polished and positive experience for customers, partners, and employees
Strong coordination across vendors, internal stakeholders, and operational event activities
Reliable execution of timelines, logistics, registrations, communications, and onsite support across multiple simultaneous events
Organized management of event budgets, materials, inventory, and deliverables
Alignment across business units and teams while maintaining a consistent Summit brand experience
Clear communication, proactive follow-through, and dependable problem-solving throughout all phases of an event

You'll Thrive in This Role If You:

Have 2–4 years of experience supporting conferences, trade shows, webinars, customer events, or related event programs
Are highly organized and comfortable managing multiple projects, timelines, budgets, and moving pieces at once
Communicate clearly and work well with both internal teams and external vendors
Can stay flexible and solution-oriented in fast-paced environments and tight deadlines
Have experience coordinating vendors, schedules, shipping, logistics, and event materials
Are comfortable using Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, and event or project management tools
Have experience supporting virtual or hybrid events using platforms such as Microsoft Teams, Zoom, or Cvent
Pay attention to the details while still keeping the bigger picture in mind
Bring a positive, collaborative attitude and are willing to jump in wherever needed
Are willing to travel for events as needed (approximately 15%)

Bonus Point:

Have supported B2B or technology industry events and trade shows
Have experience managing swag, branded materials, or event inventory
Have experience balancing competing priorities across multiple departments or event programs
Enjoy bringing structure and organization to fast-moving projects
