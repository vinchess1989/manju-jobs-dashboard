Job Title: Remote Recruitment Coordinator




Monthly Pay: $5,700 – $7,300




Summary:

The Remote Recruitment Coordinator supports hiring initiatives by managing interview scheduling, candidate communications, recruitment administration, and applicant tracking activities. This role is ideal for organized professionals looking to build a career in recruiting and talent acquisition.




Job Responsibilities:

Coordinate interviews between candidates and hiring managers.
Maintain applicant tracking system records.
Communicate with candidates throughout the hiring process.
Assist with job posting management and recruiting campaigns.
Prepare recruitment reports and hiring documentation.
Support talent acquisition projects and initiatives.




Qualifications:

Recruiting, HR, administrative, or coordination experience preferred.
Strong organizational and communication skills.
Ability to manage multiple hiring priorities.
Familiarity with applicant tracking systems is a plus.
Professional home office setup.




Perks & Benefits:

Monthly pay: $5,700–$7,300
Fully remote recruiting role
Paid onboarding and training
Career advancement opportunities
