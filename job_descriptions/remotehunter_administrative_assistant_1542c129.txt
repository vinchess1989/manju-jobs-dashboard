1. About Our Client:

The organization operates in the digital marketing and paid acquisition industry, specializing in supporting coaches, consultants, experts, and authors to achieve growth through platforms like YouTube, Facebook, TikTok, and Google. They address the challenge of scalable growth for founder-led businesses in e-learning, SaaS, and service-based internet sectors by providing hands-on ad campaign management including creative scripting, video production, and tracking. The company has experienced rapid expansion, noted for significant revenue generation and sustained growth over recent years.




2. About the Opportunity:

The Administrative Assistant role provides critical operational support across executive functions, HR, finance, and property management. This position acts as a direct proxy to the Executive Assistant and Talent Acquisition Lead, ensuring smooth daily operations and process management. It is designed to develop independent management of diverse administrative tasks, enabling senior staff to focus on strategic initiatives.




3. Responsibilities:

• Organize and maintain digital file systems and task boards

• Manage system access and administrative duties for financial tools

• Track insurance renewals and coordinate related paperwork

• Calculate and monitor CEO time expenditure

• Maintain financial trackers and assist with bank reconciliations

• Screen resumes, conduct preliminary phone interviews, and update hiring metrics

• Coordinate interview scheduling, onboarding, and offboarding processes

• Monitor rental income, vendor coordination, and maintenance expenses

• Track utility bills and assist with property documentation




4. Requirements:

• 1 to 2 years of administrative experience

• Strong problem-solving skills and the ability to learn independently

• Proficient with digital tools such as Google Drive, Asana, QuickBooks, and Workable

• Ability to manage multiple operational areas including finance, HR, and property management




5. Pay Range and Compensation Package:

• Compensation ranges from $500 to $750 per month
