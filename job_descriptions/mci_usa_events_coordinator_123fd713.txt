We are a global engagement marketing agency that creates human-centric touchpoints that unleash the power of people to deliver innovation and growth.

Firm believers in the power of building community since 1987, we are helping brands, associations and not-for-profit organisations solve their key people challenges through our core expertise: Live & virtual events, Strategic & digital communications and Consulting & community solutions.

We are an independently owned agency, headquartered in Geneva, Switzerland, with a global presence of 60 offices in 31 countries.

JOB OBJECTIVE

To serve as event coordinator for variety of association and/or corporate clients.

COMPESNATION: $27.00 - $28.85 hourly

Responsibilities

MAIN RESPONSIBILITIES


Serve in a support role to event manager on an array of events ranging from fully in-person, virtual or a hybrid model
Work with department technologies and tools, virtual event platforms, registration platforms, exhibit floor module and event mobile apps, etc.
Respond to registration email inquiries. Make registration record updates, cancellations, refunds, etc.
Maintain registration and housing tracking reports
Manage VIP rooming lists, requirements, hospitality services
Maintain department files and historical event documentation.
Source variety of event materials such as lanyards, sponsorship items, etc.
Coordinate department functions such as event supplies, shipping, event cancellation insurance, etc.
Actively participate in monthly client meetings.
Travel onsite to events, support onsite activations
Maintain department files
All other duties as assigned


Job Requirements


1-5 years of event experience
Experience in association or corporate markets and multi-client environment preferred
Experience working in Cvent and/or Map Your Show, a plus
VIP customer service, ability to deliver white glove service
Strong oral and written communication skills and ability to interact in a firm but highly collaborative fashion
Team focus but independent judgment and vision
Solutions-oriented, creative problem solver
Must have the ability to prioritize and manage multiple projects and deadlines in a fast paced, dynamically, changing environment.
Eager to learn with a desire for career growth in the events industry


PROFESSIONAL AND LEADERSHIP COMPETENCIES


Team-focused with independent judgment and vision.
Solutions-oriented and creative problem solver.
Strong interpersonal skills and ability to interact professionally with clients and colleagues.
Commitment to continuous improvement and adaptability in changing environments.


Skills

SPECIFIC TECHNICAL SKILLS


Cvent, Map Your Show or other event technologies, a plus


Other Requirements


Travel: Approximately 20%, chiefly 4-5 day blocks of time
