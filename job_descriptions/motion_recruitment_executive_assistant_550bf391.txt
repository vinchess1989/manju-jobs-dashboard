We are seeking a highly organized and proactive Executive Assistant to support two Vice Presidents within a global Web & Mobile Engineering organization. This is an initial 6-month hybrid contract opportunity.




This role is critical in ensuring smooth day-to-day operations, managing complex schedules, and helping leaders stay focused on strategic priorities in a fast-paced technology environment.







Contract: 6-months (possibility of extension)

Location: Phoenix, AZ

Hybrid




Key Responsibilities

Manage complex executive calendars across multiple time zones, balancing competing priorities and rapidly changing schedules
Coordinate meetings, travel arrangements, and logistics for senior leadership
Support team operations and organizational initiatives across a global engineering organization
Schedule and coordinate candidate interviews and recruitment activities
Maintain organizational resources including team calendars, org charts, distribution lists, and other operational documentation
Assist with special projects and provide ad hoc administrative support as needed
Serve as a trusted partner to leadership, exercising sound judgment and discretion when handling confidential information

Qualifications

Previous experience supporting senior executives, Vice Presidents, or leadership teams within a large corporate environment
Exceptional organizational skills with strong attention to detail
Ability to manage multiple priorities and adapt quickly in a fast-paced setting
Strong verbal and written communication skills
Professional demeanor with excellent judgment and problem-solving abilities
Proficiency with Microsoft Office Suite, macOS, and Slack
Experience coordinating across global teams and multiple time zones is highly preferred
