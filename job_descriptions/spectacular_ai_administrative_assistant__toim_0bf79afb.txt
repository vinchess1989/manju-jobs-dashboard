Administrative Assistant (Toimistosihteeri)
Espoo, Uusimaa

Location: Espoo (In-person)
Commitment: Part-time (2 days / week)
Start Date: August or September 2026




Spectacular AI is currently a small, highly technical team specializing in drone navigation technology. As we grow, we need a reliable part-time Administrative Assistant to help the management with essential paperwork such as invoicing, time sheets, receipts, and potentially participate in other business functions such as customer support.

Core Responsibilities

Finance & Accounting: Sending out invoices to customers, processing receipts and bills in Procountor, and keeping track of expenses.
HR Support: Managing staff benefits (like lunch vouchers), keeping track of holidays and sick leaves, and reporting these to our payroll service.
General Help: Assisting with document management, travel bookings, meeting arrangements, and occasional customer support tasks as needed.

What We Offer

A relaxed but professional small-team environment.
The chance to be a key part of our growth from a small startup to a larger team.
Flexible part-time work and competitive pay.

Requirements

Computer Skills: You are comfortable using a laptop and basic tools like email, Google Docs, Excel, Slack, video calls, and administrative web services (for example, Procountor)
Fluent in English and Finnish.
Readiness to relocate to the Helsinki region, Finland for in-person work in our Espoo office.
Legal right to work in the EU. 

Ideal Extras

The following skills, qualifications and experience are not mandatory but highly valued

Earlier experience or education (AMK, BSc, or MSc) in a relevant field like business, HR, or accounting. Especially prior experience in similar roles.
A proactive attitude — you enjoy keeping things organized and helping out where needed.
Technical abilities: good computer skills (better than the minimum required level) and general interest in technology are counted as a plus.
