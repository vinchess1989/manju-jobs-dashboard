Position: Legal Specialist

Type: Contract

Location: Remote

Commitment: 10-40 hrs/week




Role Responsibilities

Conduct thorough contract review, drafting, and negotiation to optimize business outcomes while managing risk.
Analyze cases and perform in-depth legal research to provide actionable insights and recommendations.
Support litigation and dispute resolution by preparing documentation and assisting internal and external counsel.
Assess regulatory compliance and assist with due diligence for corporate matters, mergers, and acquisitions.
Design system prompts and rubrics for legal applications, engaging in multi-turn conversations with agents to ensure task accuracy and coherence.
Uphold the highest standards of confidentiality and professional ethics in all legal processes and communications.




Requirements

Have proven expertise in legal principles, corporate law, and regulatory compliance.
Demonstrate strong contract review and negotiation abilities across a variety of legal domains.
Have demonstrated experience in case analysis, risk assessment, and due diligence.
Possess hands-on experience in litigation support and dispute resolution.
Exhibit exceptional written and verbal communication skills, with a keen attention to detail and clarity.
