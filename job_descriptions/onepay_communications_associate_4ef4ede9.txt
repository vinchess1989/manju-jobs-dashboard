The Role

Role

As a Communications Associate, you will support and execute integrated communications workstreams that shape how OnePay shows up as a company and brand — both internally and externally. The role will span employee communications, corporate social channels, company events, and media relations, helping ensure a consistent, compelling voice across all audiences. This role will work closely with the Brand / Marketing, Legal & Compliance, and People team(s) to manage everything from internal announcements, company all hands / town halls, presentations, briefings, and more. You will:


Help coordinate all internal communications (org-specific and company-wide) at OnePay
Help manage the execution of company-wide meetings, e.g., OnePay Live, (gathering and producing materials, coordinating with presenters, informing stakeholders, etc.)
Manage and execute on company’s social media content for LinkedIn and X, including drafting of copy, generation of creative assets, approval of content with relevant parties including product and legal, etc
Support employee engagement initiatives, including company-wide Slack channels, new hire orientation, external speaker series, offsites and in-person gatherings, and more
Serve as liaison between Corporate Affairs and other company stakeholders and teams
Produce presentations, talking points, and write-ups for company leaders
Produce internal and external communications strategies for company events & leaders
Help oversee a variety of administrative workstreams, including employee events, policies, and company offsites


You Bring


The ability to operate in uncertainty with a “get-stuff-done” mentality
A strong work ethic and passion for day-to-day tasks
Willingness to collaborate and partner with individuals and teams across the business
Receives feedback well and converts feedback quickly into action
Strong verbal and written communication skills.
Flexibility in a dynamic, fast-paced environment and ability to work well under pressure
Drive and proactivity - everyone here is a builder and executor
