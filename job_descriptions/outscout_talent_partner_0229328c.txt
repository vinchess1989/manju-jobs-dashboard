Räätälöi ansioluetteloni

We are looking for a Talent Partner to join our remote US-based team.




As a Talent Partner, you will be the bridge between our clients and top-tier talent, working closely with both sides to ensure high-quality matches that drive long-term success. You’ll use OutScout’s platform and data alongside your market knowledge, recruitment expertise, and relationship-building skills to deliver an outstanding hiring experience.




Key elements of the role:




Partner with hiring managers, founders, and executives to understand their business goals, team needs, and role requirements across GTM, operations, finance, engineering, and product roles
Build and manage strong candidate pipelines through proactive sourcing, referrals, and leveraging OutScout’s proprietary platform.
Kicking off searches quickly, efficiently, and accurately.
Provide market insights and data-driven recommendations to clients on talent availability, competitive benchmarking, and hiring strategies.
Guide candidates through the full recruitment lifecycle, from first conversation to offer acceptance, ensuring a transparent and positive experience.




What We’re Looking For:




Proven experience as a recruiter. Ideally, 1-4 years of recruiting experience, either in-house or at an agency.
Strong understanding of the tech landscape, emerging skills, and talent market trends.
Exceptional communication and relationship-building skills, with the ability to influence and advise stakeholders at all levels.
A proactive, problem-solving mindset with a passion for helping companies scale and candidates grow their careers.
Ability to manage multiple searches and client relationships simultaneously while maintaining high quality.
Previous experience supporting startups, scaleups, or enterprise customers is a strong plus.




You will get exposure to high-value, high-growth technology companies, supporting founders and execs, solving very difficult problems, and who require very reliable, robust, and fast service levels.




We offer a strong base salary (depending on your experience/level), quarterly bonuses with great uncapped earning potential tied to hiring and customer success, remote working, stock options, and healthcare. We have team off-sites across the US throughout the year to bring the team together and meet customers.
