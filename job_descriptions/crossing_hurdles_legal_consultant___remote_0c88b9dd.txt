Position: Legal Expert

Type: Contract (Part-time)

Compensation: $40 - $110/hour

Location: Remote

Commitment: 10-40 hrs/week




Role Responsibilities

Produce high-quality 'golden response' contracts and policies for AI training.
Collaborate with cross-functional teams to translate legal insights into actionable AI requirements.
Oversee the quality and accuracy of AI outputs, providing expert feedback to enhance performance.
Ensure compliance with regulatory standards and best practices in contract management.




Requirements

Possess a Juris Doctor (JD) degree from an ABA-accredited institution and have active bar admission in at least one U.S. jurisdiction.
Have a minimum of of transactional or compliance legal experience, demonstrating expertise in template-intensive workflows.
Demonstrate advanced proficiency in regulatory compliance, contract drafting, negotiation, and corporate law.
Have outstanding written and verbal communication skills with meticulous attention to detail.
