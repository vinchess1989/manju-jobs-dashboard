We are looking for an experienced transaction lawyer to join YIT Group Legal and cover legal matters relating to our Joint Venture transactions and operations and cross-border real estate transactions across the Baltic countries, Czech Republic, Slovakia, and Poland. The role sits within Group Legal, with a dotted line reporting to the CEE Investment Team - in practice, you will work side by side with our investment professionals from CEE Investment team and local YIT teams on deals as they progress, collaborating closely with the Group functions to make sure transactions are structured consistently with Group requirements.

The role calls for genuine involvement in transactions across all stages. A commercial outlook is important - an ability to understand the business context, engage constructively with deal teams and Group functions, and focus on practical outcomes alongside legal risk.

The role is based in Poland, Slovakia or Czech Republic. You should expect to travel within the region regularly, as well as to YIT's headquarters in Helsinki, Finland - roughly 10-20% of your time, which in practice means a few trips a month, We are open to discussing different employment models. If you are the right person for the role, we will find a setup that works.

What you'll do

Handle legal work on joint ventures and cross-border real estate transactions across the CEE region - from initial structuring through due diligence, negotiation, and closing
Work directly with the CEE Investment Team and local YIT team throughout each deal
Ensure high quality of joint venture agreements, shareholders' agreements, construction contracts, land purchase agreements, and other real estate contracts as well as timely closing of transactions in cooperation with selected professional external transaction lawyers and our local in-house lawyers
Ensure alignment with group-level requirements and policies
Advise business stakeholders on structuring options, risk, and contractual matters in clear, practical terms
Instruct and manage local external counsel responsible for drafting and negotiating of transaction documentation across the CEE region, keeping quality and costs in check
Collaborate with business colleagues, finance, tax, and other functions both on Group and country levels to keep legal workstreams moving in step with commercial timelines
Contribute to building out templates and legal processes for the CEE region

What helps you succeed in this role

4-5+ years of experience in transactional legal work (M&A, real estate, joint ventures or similar)
Strong hands-on experience in joint ventures, project financing, real estate transactions, and/or M&A
Experience working in an international or multi-jurisdictional environment is an advantage
Law degree (or equivalent) from Poland, Slovakia, Czech Republic or another European jurisdiction
Strong working proficiency in English; Polish, Slovak or Czech is an advantage
Approach legal work from a commercial angle - your instinct is to find a way forward, not to identify obstacles
Strong drafting and negotiation skills and you are comfortable holding your ground in complex discussions
Ability to manage multiple transactions simultaneously while maintaining quality and priorities
Comfort working in complex environments with multiple parties, competing interests, and layered structures are part of the job
You build good working relationships naturally, across different teams, and seniority levels

You understand customer needs and connect your daily work, decisions and solutions to creating customer value.

Your team

You will join the Group Legal team and work in close collaboration with the CEE Investment Team and local YIT organizations across several countries.
