HR Assistant – Work From Home

Location: UK Wide (Remote / Home Based)

Salary: £12 – £16 per hour (£23,400 – £31,200 per annum equivalent, based on a 37.5-hour week)

Job Type: Part-Time (Full-Time options also available)

Reporting To: HR Manager (Client Side)

About the Opportunity

Vita CV is currently recruiting on behalf of our client, a UK-based business building out its people operations capability. We are looking to appoint an HR Assistant on a part-time, fully remote basis. This is suited to candidates with prior HR exposure who want to develop within a structured, supportive people team.

About the Role

The HR Assistant will support day-to-day people operations including employee records, onboarding administration, HR documentation, and general team support across the employee lifecycle.

Key Responsibilities

Maintain accurate employee records and HR system data
Support new starter onboarding including paperwork, accounts, and welcome packs
Prepare HR letters, contracts, and routine documentation
Help coordinate training sessions, reviews, and HR communications
Support holiday, absence, and time-off tracking
Respond to general employee queries and signpost to relevant team members
Help maintain HR policies, handbooks, and internal documentation

What We're Looking For

Some prior HR, people operations, or administrative exposure
Strong written and verbal communication skills in English
Demonstrated discretion when handling confidential employee information
Good organisational skills with strong attention to detail
Comfortable using Microsoft Office, Google Workspace, and HR systems
Self-motivated and reliable in a fully remote environment
A stable home internet connection and a quiet workspace

Desirable (Nice to Have)

Exposure to HRIS platforms such as BambooHR, HiBob, Personio, or PeopleHR
Studying towards or completed a CIPD qualification (or equivalent)

What This Role Offers

Fully remote, UK-wide working arrangement
Flexible engagement across part-time and full-time hours
Broad exposure across the employee lifecycle
Clear pathway to develop HR expertise within a structured team

How to Apply

If this role aligns with your experience and working style, please submit your CV through this LinkedIn listing. Shortlisted candidates will be contacted by a member of the Vita CV team to discuss the next steps. Applications are reviewed on a rolling basis, and early submissions are encouraged.
