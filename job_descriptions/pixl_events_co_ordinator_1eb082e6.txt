Events Co-ordinator | PiXL | Remote | £30,000 per annum

Are you someone who loves bringing events to life and making every interaction count? Do you thrive on keeping things moving — from inbox to open day, from booking to conference? Can you hold the detail and the bigger picture at the same time?

This is about coordinating the events and programmes that support thousands of UK school leaders.




What we offer

Remote, full time, permanent role

£30,000 per annum

13% employer pension contribution and medical health plan

32 holiday days plus 8 bank holidays per year




The role

·      Coordinate day-to-day events and programmes processes, supporting the Events Manager

·      Coordinate Open Days, reporting to the Events Manager and Heads of Schools and Trust Partnerships

·      Coordinate and manage accommodation bookings across the business

·      Collect, report, and present key data to senior stakeholders

·      Act as Event Lead on agreed digital sessions and deputise at Primary and Post 16 National Conferences

·      Provide customer service support to the 4-18 team via inboxes and phone




What you'll bring

·      Experience in an events, coordination, or customer service role

·      Confidence managing multiple workstreams and shifting priorities

·      Strong organisational and administrative skills

·      Experience producing clear data reports for stakeholders

·      Excellent written and verbal communication skills

·      A proactive, solutions-focused mindset

·      Experience in Microsoft programmes - Excel, PowerPoint and Word




The character we're looking for

Beyond skill, we're looking for someone that cares about the service they provide internally and externally. You will have a member-focused mentality and be highly organised and calm under pressure.




PiXL is the UK's largest education leadership network, supporting thousands of schools and colleges to raise standards and improve life chances for young people. We are committed to building a diverse and inclusive workforce and welcome applications from candidates of all backgrounds
