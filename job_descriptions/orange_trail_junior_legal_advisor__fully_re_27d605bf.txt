Are you ready to be recognized for your expertise rather than staying behind the scenes?




Want direct access to major brands and industry-leading companies?




Looking to gain hands-on experience in a fast-moving, creative world, and be paid for your worth?




If you answered yes to the three questions above, keep reading. This could be your chance to join a Dynamic Advertising Agency in Bulgaria that has featured in Forbes, Entrepreneur etc. Orange Trail is looking for a Junior Legal Advisor to join its fasting growing international team.




What the ideal candidate looks like:

Proven experience in corporate, commercial, or advertising law (agency or media experience is a strong advantage)
Emotional intelligence and knowing how to read the room
Excellent knowledge of contract law, IP rights, and regulatory compliance
Ability to work across multiple projects simultaneously in a fast-paced environment
Strong problem-solving mindset with a practical, business-oriented approach
Exceptional communication skills, and to be able to translate legal complexity into clear, actionable advice
High level of responsiveness and ownership, with the ability to act quickly when urgent matters arise
Comfortable working in a dynamic, deadline-driven setting with shifting priorities
Strong attention to detail while maintaining a big-picture perspective
Fluency in English/Bulgarian (written and spoken)
Qualified legal professional (LLB/LLM or equivalent), admitted to practice in Bulgaria or the EU




What you’ll do:

Advise on contracts, partnerships, and advertising regulations
Support creative teams with clear, practical legal guidance
Manage risk while enabling innovation
Ensure compliance in a rapidly evolving industry
Create and share legal documents such as contracts, NDAs, non-competes, and more for clients, partners and employees
Advise the executive and leadership team on best practice methodology for legal matters
Filing of necessary legal paperwork
Resolving client disputes 
Registering and advising on entities for the company
Day to day legal consultations for operations and finance team




Why join us:

 🚀 Work in a high-energy, fully remote environment
🌍 Gain exposure to major clients and partners such as Tiktok, Google, Microsoft & more
⚖️ Combine legal expertise with real business influence
📈 Accelerate your growth with diverse, hands-on experience
🤝 Collaborate with ambitious, talented professionals from 4 continents




If you’re ready to move fast, think big, and make your mark, this is your place.
