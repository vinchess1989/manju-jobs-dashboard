The Role

We are seeking a motivated and detail-oriented Event Specialist to join our Marketing team, reporting to the Brand & Events Manager. This is an exciting opportunity for someone early in their events career who is eager to get hands-on experience across the full event lifecycle, from trade shows and conferences to webinars and internal events.

The ideal candidate is highly organized, thrives in a fast-paced environment, and brings a collaborative spirit and genuine enthusiasm for creating experiences that drive awareness, pipeline, and customer engagement.

What You'll Do


Support the planning and execution of 20–40 events per year, including trade shows, industry conferences, sponsored events, customer dinners, webinars, and internal company events.
Coordinate event logistics end-to-end: including vendor management, shipping, swag, and on-site execution.
Manage webinar production using platforms such as Zoom, including setup, speaker coordination, run-of-show, and post-event follow-up.
Collaborate closely with Marketing, Sales, Product Marketing, and Growth Engineering teams to align event programming with campaign goals and business priorities.
Support pre- and post-event marketing activities, including promotional emails, landing pages, and follow-up sequences in HubSpot.
Track event performance metrics and contribute to reporting on ROI, pipeline influence, and engagement outcomes.
Maintain event calendars, project timelines, and internal documentation to keep stakeholders aligned and deadlines on track.
Support the Brand & Events Manager in developing strategies that serve brand awareness, thought leadership, pipeline generation, and customer retention goals.
Leverage AI tools to build efficiency into event planning workflows, content drafting, and communications.
Contribute to broader marketing initiatives as needed, including social media promotion, event-related content, and campaign support.


Who You Are

Must-haves


Bachelor’s degree and 1–3 years of experience in event coordination, marketing, or a related field.
Strong organizational and project management skills, with the ability to manage multiple events and deadlines simultaneously.
Excellent written and verbal communication skills, with a keen eye for detail and brand consistency.
Experience with or strong aptitude for marketing and event platforms, including HubSpot, Zoom, or similar tools.
Comfortable working cross-functionally with Sales, Product, and Executive teams to align event efforts with business goals.
Data-driven mindset — able to track event metrics and contribute to performance reporting.
Ability to manage vendor and supplier relationships
Willingness to travel occasionally for on-site event support.


Nice-to-haves


Experience with design tools such as Canva or Adobe Creative Suite for producing event materials and promotional assets.
Familiarity with AI productivity tools such as Claude to build efficiency into event planning workflows, content drafting, and communications.
Proven ability to support events that contribute measurably to pipeline or brand metrics.
Experience in hospitality is a plus.
