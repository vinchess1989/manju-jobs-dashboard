Job description
Join the JetDogs as a Marketing Intern!
Hey there, fellow game lover! We're JetDogs, a cozy game studio nestled in Helsinki, Finland - and guess what? We're on the hunt for a fresh, eager Marketing Intern to hop into our virtual playground. 🎮✨

Here's the scoop:
You'll share promotional content, updates, and campaigns across Steam, mobile stores, and other platforms.
You'll manage and grow our Facebook and Steam groups.
You'll plan and execute discounts, special offers, bundles, and sales campaigns.
Get creative with AI tools to design visuals, banners, social posts, and marketing materials.
Forget the standard 9-to-5 - work the hours that fit your life, as long as the job gets done.
Remote work? You bet! But if you're in Helsinki, we'd love to occasionally high-five in person.

And just so we're clear up front: unfortunately, this is an unpaid position - but it's packed with real experience, learning, portfolio-building opportunities, and genuine game industry fun.

What we're looking for in you:
You're studying marketing, digital marketing, communications, game development, or just tossed your grad cap into the air.
You're a creative thinker who loves engaging with communities and has an eye for detail.
Eager to experiment with AI tools and graphic design software to create compelling content.
Independence is your middle name, but you're also a rockstar in a team.
Communicating in English? Piece of cake for you.


Bonus points if you:
Have dipped your toes into social media management, community building, or indie game marketing.
Know your way around Steam communities, Facebook groups, and running promotions/discount campaigns.
Are a gaming enthusiast with a soft spot for casual and indie games.
Have a small portfolio of graphics, posts, or marketing ideas you can show us.

Oh, and did we mention? We've got a delightful array of projects in the works - from chill PC games to charming console puzzles - that need your creative spark to reach the right players!

Ready to level up with us? Let's make games (and their marketing) that players will love and talk about! 🕹️🌟

Apply now and let's create some fun!
