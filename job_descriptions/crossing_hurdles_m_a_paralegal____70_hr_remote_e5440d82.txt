Position: Corporate M&A Paralegal

Type: Contract

Compensation: $40 - $70/hour

Location: Remote

Commitment: 10-40 hrs/week




Role Responsibilities

Support attorneys and deal teams in all stages of M&A transactions, including due diligence, document preparation, and post-closing activities.
Review, organize, and manage sensitive corporate and financial documents, ensuring strict compliance with Personally Identifiable Information (PII) regulations.
Oversee secure handling, redaction, and transfer of PII and other confidential data throughout deal processes.
Assist in drafting, filing, and maintaining compliance documentation and regulatory submissions as required.
Conduct PII compliance checks, identify risks, and implement process improvements to enhance data privacy.
Liaise with internal stakeholders and external parties, clearly communicating updates, risks, and requirements both in writing and verbally.




Requirements:

Have strong experience with PII compliance in a corporate or M&A legal setting.
Demonstrate expertise in handling and protecting sensitive and proprietary documentation.
Possess strong knowledge of Mergers & Acquisitions processes, terminology, and best practices.
Have a deep understanding of regulatory frameworks governing data privacy and compliance.
Have a proven ability to manage complex financial documents and diligence materials.
