Job Description


We are looking for an Mergers & Acquisition Analyst to join Konecranes’ Corporate Mergers & Acquisitions team. This role offers an excellent opportunity to build hands‑on M&A experience in a global industrial company.

You will work closely with experienced M&A professionals and senior leaders, supporting transactions and strategic investment decisions that shape the future of Konecranes.

Location: Konecranes is headquartered in Hyvinkää, Finland. This role is primarily based at our modern offices in Keilaranta, Espoo, where the team is located. We work in a hybrid model and enjoy collaborating in the office, with around 50% of your working time spent on-site. Occasional travel is part of the role. Travel is limited in the beginning (approximately 10 days per year, depending on project needs) and may increase over time.

Key responsibilities:

Support in evaluating investment opportunities, including market, financial, and strategic analysis.
Participate in due diligence processes across financial, commercial, tax, legal, HR and operational areas.
Prepare valuation models (DCF, multiples-based valuation, scenario analyses).
Assist in the preparation of investment proposals, presentations, and internal decision materials.
Conduct industry and competitor research to support M&A pipeline development.
Coordinate with internal stakeholders (finance, legal, business units) and external advisors.

Qualifications


We are looking for a structured and hands‑on professional who enjoys working in an ambitious environment. You are motivated to learn and take responsibility, while working closely with experienced colleagues. You are comfortable working with numbers and complex topics to support business decisions, and you approach your work with a collaborative mindset.

What we expect:

Approximately 1-5 years of relevant work experience in M&A, transaction services, audit, business controlling, due diligence or business development.
A relevant master's degree in finance, accounting, economics or a related field.
Solid understanding and ability to evaluate financial information.
Financial modelling skills, including experience with DCF, multiples‑based valuation and scenario analysis.
Strong analytical skills and the ability to support the analysis of complex problems using data and financial information.
Ability to work independently while collaborating effectively with multiple stakeholders across functions in a multinational environment.
Excellent verbal and written communication skills in English; additional languages are an advantage.
Experience in an industrial or international material handling business environment is a plus.

Additional Information


Why join us?

This role offers a unique opportunity to learn hands‑on M&A work in a global industrial company. You will work closely with experienced professionals who are committed to supporting your development and sharing their expertise. As part of the Corporate M&A team, you will gain exposure to real investment projects and contribute to analyses and materials that support senior management decision‑making. The role provides a strong overview of how strategic transactions are evaluated and executed in an international business environment.
